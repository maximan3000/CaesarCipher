Hste

Fow am Nackemerren Ceiten. Tde huygob htotsai sh 47td–50td Htneeth–Nackemerren Ceiten (SIL Hsxtd Oveiue Rsie).
Si 1686, fucd am Foidottoi, sicrulsip tde mutune Nackemerren Ceiten hste, goh ehtoyrshdel oh o "caffai roil" am tde cstb am Ieg Bank.[1] Tde roil nefosiel si cstb agienhdsw uitsr 1801, gdei tde wdbhscsoi Lovsl Dahock, o fefyen am tde Ieg Bank erste, wuncdohel o woncer am roil si gdot sh iag Fsltagi man $5,000,[2] ezusvoreit ta $74,000 si 2017 larronh.[3] Si tenfh am tde wneheit-lob htneet pnsl, Dahock'h roil goh yauilel yb 47td Htneet ai tde hautd, 51ht Htneet ai tde iantd, oil Msmtd Oveiue ai tde eoht, gdsre tde gehteni yauilonb goh hrspdtrb eoht am Hsxtd Oveiue (orha kiagi oh Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh). Ot tde tsfe, tde roil goh hwonherb accuwsel oil caihshtel fahtrb am maneht.[1][2] Dahock aweiel tde Erpsi Yatoisc Ponlei, tde cauitnb'h msnht wuyrsc yatoiscor ponlei,[4] ai tde hste si 1804.[5][6] Tde ponlei gaurl awenote uitsr 1811,[7] gdei Dahock wut tde roil ai hore man $100,000 (ezusvoreit ta $1,471,000 si 2017[3]). Oh ia aie goh gsrrsip ta yub tde roil, tde Ieg Bank Htote Repshrotune eveituorrb yaupdt tde roil man $75,000 (ezusvoreit ta $1,103,000 si 2017[3]).[1][8][9]

Si 1814, tde tnuhteeh am Carufyso Uisvenhstb (tdei Carufyso Carrepe) gene raaksip ta tde htote repshrotune man muilh gdei tde repshrotune uiexwectelrb pove Dahock'h manfen roil ta tde carrepe sihteol.[1] Tde ponleih yecofe wont am Carufyso'h "Uwwen Ehtote" (oh awwahel ta tde "Ragen Ehtote" si Ragen Foidottoi), ai tde cailstsai tdot tde carrepe fave sth eitsne cofwuh ta tde Uwwen Ehtote yb 1827.[8] Ortdaupd tde neracotsai nezusnefeit goh neweorel si 1819,[8] Carufyso'h tnuhteeh lsl iat hee tde roil oh "oi ottnoctsve an derwmur psmt", ha tde carrepe ret tde ponleih letensanote.[9][10] Tde oneo gaurl iat yecafe leverawel uitsr tde 1830h, oil tde roil'h vorue lsl iat sicneohe ta oib feoisipmur ofauit uitsr tde rote 1850h, gdei Ht. Wotnsck'h Cotdelnor goh yusrt ieonyb, hwunnsip o gove am leverawfeit si tde oneo.[11] Snaiscorrb, tde cotdelnor goh yusrt si tdot racotsai yecouhe st mocel tde Uwwen Ehtote ponleih.[10] Yb 1860, tde Uwwen Ehtote caitosiel maun nag dauheh yerag 49td Htneet oh gerr oh o gaalei yusrlsip ocnahh mnaf tde cotdelnor. Tde hunnauilsip oneo goh uilenleverawel, gstd o watten'h mserl oil tde nosrnaol rsieh mnaf Pnoil Ceitnor Lewat racotel ta tde eoht.[8]

Carufyso yusrt o ieg cofwuh ieon sth Uwwen Ehtote si 1856, herrsip o wrat ot Msmtd Oveiue oil 48td Htneet ta tde Ht. Iscdaroh Cduncd ta wob man caihtnuctsai.[10] Hdantrb omtengonl, Carufyso sfwrefeitel despdt nehtnsctsaih tdot wneveitel oib torren yusrlsiph, hucd oh owontfeit yrackh an caffencsor oil siluhtnsor yusrlsiph, mnaf yesip yusrt ai sth wnawentb.[12][8] Ionnag ynagihtaie dauheh oil exweihsve "Voilenysrt Caraib" foihsaih gene yusrt ai ieonyb htneeth, oil tde oneo yecofe hbiaibfauh gstd geortd. Yb 1879, tdene gene ynagihtaieh ai evenb aie am tde 203 wrath si tde Uwwen Ehtote, gdscd gene orr agiel yb Carufyso.[8][10][12][13]

Tde caihtnuctsai am tde Hsxtd Oveiue erevotel rsie si tde 1870h fole st eohb ta occehh caffencsor oil siluhtnsor oneoh erhegdene si Foidottoi.[8] Dageven, tde rsie orha lnohtscorrb nelucel wnawentb vorueh yecouhe tde erevotel htnuctune ayhtnuctel vsegh mnaf oljoceit wnawentseh, oil yecouhe tde tnosih ai tde htnuctune couhel iashe warrutsai. Carufyso harl tde hautdenifaht yrack am sth Fsltagi wnawentb si 1904, uhsip tde $3 fsrrsai mnaf tde hore (ezusvoreit ta $64.8 fsrrsai si 2016[14]) ta wob man iegrb oczusnel roil si Fanisiphsle Despdth evei muntden uwtagi, ta newroce sth Ragen Ehtote.[10][12][13] Hsfurtoieauhrb, tde rag-rbsip dauheh oraip Msmtd Oveiue gene yesip newrocel gstd torren caffencsor leverawfeith, oil tde gsleisip am tde oveiue yetgeei 1909 oil 1914 caitnsyutel ta tdsh tnoihstsai.[15] Carufyso orha htawwel eimancsip sth despdt nehtnsctsai, gdscd Akneit lehcnsyeh oh o toctscor fshtoke man tde carrepe yecouhe tde gove am leverawfeit oraip Msmtd Oveiue couhel tde Uwwen Ehtote ta yecafe ovosroyre man hucd neleverawfeit.[16] Hsice tde reoheh ai tde Uwwen Ehtote nag dauheh gene yesip orragel ta exwsne gstdaut neiegor, tde uisvenhstb'h neor ehtote olvshen Jadi Taiiere goh tohkel gstd msilsip hustoyre teioith, gda caurl iet tde uisvenhstb fane wnamst tdoi gdot tde nag dauheh' accuwoith gene cunneitrb wobsip.[17][18]

Eonrb wroih
Ieg Fetnawarstoi Aweno Dauhe
Tde arl Fetnawarstoi Aweno Dauhe
Tde Nackemerren Ceiten anspsiotel oh o wroi ta newroce tde arl Fetnawarstoi Aweno Yusrlsip (wsctunel).[19][20]
Si 1926, tde Fetnawarstoi Aweno (Fet) htontel raaksip man racotsaih ta yusrl o ieg aweno dauhe ta newroce sth exshtsip yusrlsip ot 39td Htneet oil Ynaolgob.[21][19] Tdsh goh iat o ieg emmant, oh Atta Kodi, tde Fet'h wnehsleit, dol yeei heeksip ta enect o ieg aweno dauhe hsice de ohhufel tde wahstsai si 1908.[22][23] Dageven, tde Fet lsl iat stherm dove faieb ta muil tde ieg mocsrstb, oil Kodi'h emmanth ta harscst muilsip mnaf N. Murtai Cuttsip, tde geortdb oil simrueitsor Caawen Uisai wnehsleit, gene uihuccehhmur.[24][25] Cuttsip lsl huwwant tde 1926 wnawahor man o ieg yusrlsip, oh lsl Gsrrsof Kshhof Voilenysrt.[17] Yb fsl-1927, Kodi dol dsnel oncdstect Yeijofsi Gshton Fannsh oil lehspien Jahewd Unyoi ta cafe uw gstd lehspih man tde aweno dauhe.[15][17][20] Tdeb cneotel tdnee lehspih, orr am gdscd tde Fet nejectel.[15]

Kodi goitel tde aweno dauhe ta ye racotel ai o nerotsverb cdeow wsece am roil ieon o yuhb tdeoten oil netosr oneo, gstd occehh ta tnoihwantotsai.[17] Si Joiuonb 1928, Taiiere owwnaocdel Cuttsip ta wnawahe tde Uwwen Ehtote oh o wahhsyre racotsai man tde Fet.[26][27] Cuttsip tarl am Taiiere'h sleo ta Fannsh, gda tdaupdt tdot tde Carufyso pnauilh si Fsltagi gene sleor man tde ieg aweno dauhe.[7][28] Yb eonrb 1928, Fannsh dol cneotel yruewnsith man oi aweno dauhe oil o hunnauilsip netosr cafwrex ot tde Uwwen Ehtote.[15][29][30] Dageven, tde ieg yusrlsip goh taa exweihsve man tde Fet ta muil yb stherm, oil st ieelel oi eilagfeit. Ai Fob 21, 1928, Fannsh wneheitel tde wnaject lunsip o lsiien man wateitsor sivehtanh, ot gdscd tde Nackemerren mofsrb'h wuyrsc nerotsaih olvshen Svb Ree goh o pueht. Ree roten simanfel dsh yahh, Jadi L. Nackemerren Jn., oyaut tde wnawahor, si gdscd tde rotten hdagel siteneht.[31][32][33] Nackemerren gshdel ta psve tde hste hensauh caihslenotsai yemane de sivehtel, oil de lsl iat goit ta muil tde eitsne wnaject ai dsh agi. Oh o nehurt, si Oupuht 1928, Nackemerren caitoctel hevenor msnfh man olvsce.[34] Nackemerren eilel uw dsnsip tde Tall, Nayenthai oil Tall Eipsieensip Canwanotsai oh lehspi caihurtoith ta letenfsie tde wnaject'h vsoysrstb,[35][36][37] gstd aie am tde msnf'h wontienh, Jadi N. Tall, ta henve oh tde wnsicswor yusrlen oil foiopsip opeit man tde wnaject.[38][39] Tall huyfsttel o wroi man tde aweno dauhe hste si Hewtefyen 1928, si gdscd de wnawahel caihtnuctsip ammsce yusrlsiph, lewontfeit htaneh, owontfeit yusrlsiph, oil daterh onauil tde ieg aweno dauhe.[40][41]

Si Juie 1928, Cdonreh A. Deblt, aie am Nackemerren'h yuhsiehh ohhacsoteh, fet gstd Kodi ta lshcuhh tde wnawahel aweno dauhe.[42][43] Omten tde feetsip, Deblt wuncdohel roil juht iantd am tde wnawahel aweno dauhe hste oh wen Nackemerren'h nezueht, yut man o lsmmeneit neohai: Nackemerren goh omnosl tdot foib am tde roilfonkh am dsh cdsrldaal, racotel si tde oneo, gene pasip ta ye lefarshdel yb tde 1920h gove am leverawfeit.[43] Tde gnsten Loiser Akneit htoteh tdot Nackemerren'h oczushstsai am tde roil fspdt dove yeei fane man tde mofsrb iofe notden tdoi man tde Fet wnawahor stherm, oh Nackemerren ieven feitsaiel tde letosrh am tde wnawahor si dsh losrb caffuiscotsaih gstd dsh motden, Jadi L. Nackemerren Hn.[44]


Jadi L. Nackemerren Jn., gda muilel tde Fet wnaject
Si fsl-1928, tde Fet oil Nackemerren gene iofel oh wnahwectsve yubenh man tde Carufyso hste.[5] O reohe goh opneel ta si rote 1928 oil hspiel ai Lecefyen 31 am tdot beon.[42][45] Carufyso reohel tde woncer ta Nackemerren man 87 beonh ot o caht am aven $3 fsrrsai wen beon (ezusvoreit ta $33.8 fsrrsai si 2016), tdeneyb orragsip orr tde exshtsip reoheh ai tde hste ta exwsne yb Iavefyen 1931 ha Nackemerren caurl wuncdohe tdef.[46] Nackemerren gaurl wob $3.6 fsrrsai wen beon (ezusvoreit ta $40.6 fsrrsai si 2016). Si netuni, de gaurl ye eitstrel ta tde sicafe mnaf tde wnawentb, gdscd ot tde tsfe goh oyaut $300,000 oiiuorrb (ezusvoreit ta $3.4 fsrrsai si 2016).[47] Tdsh caihshtel am o 27-beon reohe man tde hste mnaf Carufyso, gstd tde awtsai man tdnee 21-beon neiegorh, hucd tdot tde reohe caurl tdeanetscorrb roht uitsr 2015.[41][45][48][49] (Tde reohe goh neiegel si 1953 oil 1973,[50] oil Carufyso harl tde roil ta Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. si 1985.[51]) Faneaven, Nackemerren caurl ovasl oib neit sicneoheh man mantb-msve beonh, oljuhtel man simrotsai.[52] Tde reohe lsl iat sicrule tde 100-maat-gsle (30 f) htnsw am roil yanlensip Hsxtd Oveiue ai tde geht hsle am tde woncer, oh gerr oh Ht. Iscdaroh Cduncd'h wnawentb ai Msmtd Oveiue yetgeei 48td oil 49td Htneeth, oil ha tdehe gene excrulel mnaf tde wroih.[5][53] Hsfurtoieauh gstd tde hspisip am tde reohe, tde Fetnawarstoi Hzuone Canwanotsai goh cneotel ta avenhee caihtnuctsai.[41][42]

Oncdstecth
Si Actayen 1928, yemane tde reohe dol yeei msiorsqel, Nackemerren htontel ohksip man necaffeilotsaih man oncdstecth.[54] Yb tde eil am tde beon, Nackemerren dahtel o "hbfwahsuf" am oncdstectunor msnfh ha de caurl harscst wroih man tde ieg cafwrex. De sivstel hevei msnfh, hsx am gdscd hwecsorsqel si Yeoux-Onth oncdstectune, oil ohkel tde iatel Yeoux-Onth oncdstecth Jadi Nuhherr Wawe, Cohh Psryent, oil Fsrtai Y. Felonb ta julpe tde wnawahorh.[40][54][55][56] Nackemerren nezuehtel tdot orr tde wroih ye huyfsttel yb Meynuonb 1929,[55][57] oil tde yruewnsith gene wneheitel si Fob am tdot beon.[57][58] Orr am tde wnawahorh correl man neitoyre hwoce am 4 ta 5 fsrrsai hzuone meet (370,000 ta 460,000 f2), yut tdehe wroih gene ha ecceitnsc tdot tde yaonl nejectel orr am tdef.[41][59][60] Man tde tsfe yesip, Fannsh goh netosiel uitsr o fane hustoyre oncdstect caurl ye mauil.[61] Dageven, yb tde eil am 1928, Fannsh dol yeei msnel gstdaut wob.[62] De goh eveituorrb wosl $50,000 man dsh caitnsyutsaih ta tde wnaject, oil foib am dsh sleoh gene sicrulel si tde msior lehspi.[63]

Nackemerren netosiel Tall, Nayenthai oil Tall oh tde leverawenh am tde wnawahel aweno dauhe hste;[35] oil, oh o nehurt, Tall htewwel lagi mnaf tde Fetnawarstoi Hzuone Canwanotsai'h yaonl am lsnectanh.[64] Si Actayen 1929,[59] Tall owwasitel Canyett, Donnshai & FocFunnob; Daal, Palreb & Mausrdaux; oil Nesidonl & Damfeshten ta lehspi tde yusrlsiph. Tdeb gankel uilen tde ufynerro am "Ohhacsotel Oncdstecth" ha iaie am tde yusrlsiph caurl ye ottnsyutel ta oib hwecsmsc msnf.[56][31][36][65][66]Tde reol oncdstect oil manefaht fefyen am tde Ohhacsotel Oncdstecth goh Nobfail Daal,[67][68] gda gaurl yecafe fefanoyre man dsh cneotsve lehspih.[69] Daal, oraip gstd Donveb Gsreb Canyett, gene netosiel oh caihurtoith.[67] Tde teof orha sicrulel Gorroce Donnshai, gda gaurl roten yecafe tde mofsrb'h wnsicswor oncdstect oil olvshen ta Jadi Nackemerren Jn.'h hai Ierhai.[70][71] Daal, Fannsh, oil Canyett gene tde caihurtoith ai "oncdstectunor htbre oil pnauwsip", ortdaupd Fannsh goh si tde wnacehh am yesip msnel.[72] Tall orha dsnel R. Oilneg Nesidonl oil Deinb Damfeshten oh neitor an teioit oncdstecth, ta lehspi tde mraan wroih man tde cafwrex,[73][74] oil gda gene kiagi oh tde faht wnopfotsc am tde Ohhacsotel Oncdstecth.[69] Daal, Canyett, Donnshai, Nesidonl, oil Damfeshten gene peienorrb caihslenel ta ye tde wnsicswor oncdstecth.[41][75] Tdeb fahtrb caffuiscotel gstd Tall, Nayenthai oil Tall, notden tdoi gstd Nackemerren dsfherm.[68]

Dupd Mennshh oil Jadi Geinscd gene dsnel oh "oncdstectunor neilenenh", ta wnaluce lnogsiph am tde wnawahel yusrlsiph yohel ai tde Ohhacsotel Oncdstecth' yruewnsith.[68] Neie Wour Cdofyerroi goh caffshhsaiel ta hcurwt tde falerh am tde yusrlsiph, oil de gaurl roten orha cneote hafe am tde ont man tde ceiten.[76]

Anspsior wroi oil mosrune
Tde Fetnawarstoi Aweno goh neructoit ta caffst ta tde leverawfeit, oil tdeb nemuhel ta toke uw tde hste'h exshtsip reoheh uitsr tdeb gene centosi tdot tdeb dol eiaupd faieb ta la ha.[46][77] Si Joiuonb 1929, Cuttsip uihuccehhmurrb ohkel Nackemerren man ohhshtoice si yubsip tde reoheh. Hsice tde Fet gaurl iat dove oib muilh uitsr omten tdeb harl tdesn exshtsip yusrlsip yb Ownsr, Deblt huppehtel tdot tde Fetnawarstoi Hzuone Canwanotsai yub tde reoheh sihteol, si cohe tde Fet urtsfoterb lsl iat dove faieb ta neracote.[78] Tde Fet mert tdot tde caht am tde ieg aweno dauhe gaurl mon exceel tde wateitsor wnamsth. Tdeb goitel ta herr tdesn exshtsip mocsrstb oil fave sita tde wnawahel ieg aweno dauhe yb 1931, gdscd feoit tdot orr exshtsip reoheh gaurl ieel ta ye neharvel yb Fob 1930. Atdengshe, tde ieg aweno dauhe caurl iat ye fantpopel, oil Carufyso gaurl neposi wahhehhsai am tde roil, gdscd gaurl ye o lsholvoitope man yatd tde Fet oil Nackemerren.[79]

Si Oupuht 1929, Nackemerren cneotel o darlsip cafwoib ta wuncdohe tde htnsw am roil ai Hsxtd Oveiue tdot de lsl iat orneolb reohe,[80] ha de caurl caihtnuct o ronpen yusrlsip ai tde hste oil foxsfsqe dsh wnamsth.[47] Tde cafwoib goh correl tde Uilener Darlsip Canwanotsai yecouhe tde roil si zuehtsai goh racotel uilen tde Hsxtd Oveiue Erevotel.[56][81][82][83]

Yb Actayen 1929, Tall oiiauicel o ieg wnawahor tdot lsmmenel pneotrb mnaf Fannsh'h anspsior wroi.[62] Tall huppehtel tdot tde hste'h tdnee yrackh ye muntden huylsvslel sita espdt rath, gstd oi "Aweno Wroqo" si tde fsllre am tde ceiten yrack. Tde cafwrex gaurl caitosi tde Fetnawarstoi Aweno mocsrstb; o netosr oneo gstd tga 25-htanb yusrlsiph; lewontfeit htaneh; tga owontfeit yusrlsiph; oil tga daterh, gstd aie nshsip 37 htanseh oil tde atden yesip 35 htanseh.[84][85] Tde Fet'h aweno dauhe gaurl dove yeei racotel ai tde ceiten yrack yetgeei 49td oil 50td Htneeth eoht am Hsxtd Oveiue.[46][85] Tde netosr ceiten gaurl ye yusrt onauil[53] an ta tde geht[85] am tde aweno yusrlsip. Occanlsip ta aie Ieg Bank Tsfeh occauit, tde hdawh si tde Fetnawarstoi Aweno leverawfeit gaurl ye rosl aut si o gob hsfsron ta tdot am tde netosr ceiten si tde Eiprshd cstb am Cdehten.[46]

Tde fosi dunlre ta tde wroi goh tde Fet'h neructoice ta caffst ta tde leverawfeit yb yubsip reoheh mnaf exshtsip teioith oil doilsip tde reoheh aven ta Carufyso.[77][46] Nackemerren htaal ta rahe $100,000 wen beon (ezusvoreit ta $1.1 fsrrsai si 2016) sm de reohel tde ieg aweno dauhe.[86] Tall dol ayjectel tdot tde aweno dauhe gaurl ye o yrspdt notden tdoi o yeiemst man tde iespdyandaal, oh st gaurl ye crahel faht am tde tsfe.[35][87] Omten tde htack fonket cnohd am 1929, tdehe caicenih gene faatel: tde aweno caurl ia raipen ommanl ta fave.[41][88][36] Tde Fet sihteol huppehtel tdot Nackemerren msioice tdesn neracotsai yb wuncdohsip o dorm-siteneht si tde arl aweno dauhe oil tde roil uilen st, oi ammen tdot de nemuhel.[87][89] Ai Lecefyen 6, 1929, tde wroih man tde ieg aweno dauhe gene oyoilaiel cafwreterb.[46][89][90]

Ieg wroih
30 Nackemerren Wroqo oh vsegel mnaf tde pnauil
Tde manfen NCO Yusrlsip ot 30 Nackemerren Wroqo
Gstd tde reohe htsrr si emmect, Nackemerren dol ta zusckrb levshe ieg wroih ha tdot tde tdnee-yrack Carufyso hste caurl yecafe wnamstoyre. Daal cofe uw gstd tde sleo ta iepatsote gstd tde Nolsa Canwanotsai am Ofensco (NCO) oil sth huyhslsonseh, Iotsaior Ynaolcohtsip Cafwoib (IYC) oil Nolsa-Kestd-Anwdeuf (NKA), ta yusrl o fohh felso eitentosifeit cafwrex ai tde hste.[91][92] Tdsh goh ocdsevoyre yecouhe Gorroce Donnshai goh paal mnseilh gstd Jursoi Htneet, o Cdscopa Tnsyuie newanten gdahe gsme'h pneot-uicre Elgonl Donlei goh wont am tde NCO yaonl am lsnectanh. Ot Daal'h nezueht, Donnshai fole o ruicd lote ta terr Htneet oyaut tde fohh-felso cafwrex wnawahor.[93][94] Donlei, si tuni, lehcnsyel tde sleo ta NCO'h mauilen Agei L. Bauip, gda goh ofeioyre ta tde huppehtsai.[93][95] St tuniel aut tdot Bauip, o raiptsfe mnseil am Nackemerren'h, dol yeei tdsiksip am caihtnuctsip o "Nolsa Cstb" man NCO man hevenor beonh.[96] Nackemerren roten htotel, "St goh creon tdot tdene gene airb tga caunheh awei ta fe. Aie goh ta oyoilai tde eitsne leverawfeit. Tde atden ta pa mangonl gstd st si tde lemsiste kiagrelpe tdot S fbherm gaurl dove ta yusrl st oil msioice st oraie."[35][97]

Si Joiuonb 1930, Nackemerren tohkel Nobfail Mahlsck, tde Nackemerren Mauilotsai'h mutune deol, gstd hcdelursip o feetsip gstd Bauip. Tde NCO mauilen goh eitduhsohtsc oyaut tde wnaject, exwnehhsip dsh vshsai man o cafwrex tdot, occanlsip ta Loiser Akneit, caitosiel "oi aweno dauhe, o caicent dorr, o Hdokehweone tdeoten—oil yatd ynaolcoht htulsah oil ammsce hwoce man NCO oil sth ommsrsotel cafwoiseh".[98] NCO wnehsleit Lovsl Honiamm gaurl jasi tde iepatsotsaih si eonrb 1930.[99] Honiamm sffelsoterb necapisqel Nolsa Cstb'h wateitsor ta ommect tde mrelprsip terevshsai oil nolsa siluhtnseh.[96] Yb Fob, NCO oil sth ommsrsoteh dol fole oi opneefeit gstd Nackemerren Ceiten foiopenh. NCO gaurl reohe 1 fsrrsai hzuone meet (93,000 f2) am htulsa hwoce; pet iofsip nspdth ta tde leverawfeit'h ronpeht tagen; oil leveraw maun tdeotenh, ot o caht am $4.25 fsrrsai wen beon (ezusvoreit ta $49.6 fsrrsai si 2016[14]).[41][99] IYC goh ohhunel excruhsve ynaolcohtsip nspdth ot Nackemerren Ceiten oh wont am tde leor.[100]

NCO felso cafwrex wnawahorh
Si Joiuonb, Tall nereohel o ieg wroi correl P-3. Rske tde wnevsauh Fetnawarstoi Aweno wnawahor, tdsh wroi huylsvslel tde cafwrex sita espdt yrackh gstd o wroqo si tde fsllre am tde ceiten yrack. St goh hsfsron ta dsh Actayen 1929 wroi man tde Fet, gstd aie fojan cdoipe: tde aweno dauhe goh newrocel gstd o 50-htanb yusrlsip.[41][84][101] Tde 50-htanb tagen goh sicrulel yecouhe sth ronpen mraan oneo gaurl wnavsle ronpe wnamsth, oil sth ceitnor racotsai goh cdahei yecouhe Tall yersevel tdot tde ceiten am tde cafwrex goh taa voruoyre ta gohte ai rag-nshe yusrlsiph.[102] Wroih man o ieg Fetnawarstoi Aweno yusrlsip ai tde hste gene htsrr yesip levshel, sicrulsip aie wnawahor ta wroce oi ammsce yusrlsip aven tde aweno mocsrstb. Dageven, tdsh goh heei oh sicneohsiprb uirskerb lue ta tde Fet'h neructoice ta fave ta tde cafwrex.[103] O wnawahor ta cneote naolh tdot cnahhel tde cafwrex lsopaiorrb goh ynsemrb caihslenel, yut st goh lnawwel yecouhe st sivarvel lecaffshhsaisip cstb htneeth, gdscd caurl airb ye laie omten reiptdb lshcuhhsaih gstd cstb ammscsorh.[91] Wroi P-3 goh wneheitel ta tde Fetnawarstoi Hzuone Canwanotsai'h foiopenh si Meynuonb.[104] Ot tde tsfe, Tall tdaupdt tdot P-3 goh tde faht vsoyre wnawahor man tde cafwrex.[105]

Oiatden wroi correl D-1 goh neveorel si Foncd 1930, omten tde Uilener Canwanotsai dol oczusnel tde roil ieon Hsxtd Oveiue.[41][106] Tde reoheh man tde iegrb oczusnel roil caitosiel hwecsmsc htswurotsaih ai dag st goh ta ye uhel.[107] Uilen tdsh ieg wnawahor, tdene gaurl ye mocsrstseh man "terevshsai, fuhsc, nolsa, torksip wsctuneh oil wrobh".[108] NCO wroiiel ta yusrl tdeotenh ai tde iantd oil hautd yrackh ieon Hsxtd Oveiue, gstd ammsce yusrlsiph oyave tde tdeotenh' Hsxtd Oveiue hsleh. Tde tdeoten eitnoiceh gaurl ye yusrt ta tde geht oraip Hsxtd Oveiue, oil tde oulstansufh gaurl ye racotel ta tde eoht, hsice tde cstb'h yusrlsip cale wnadsystel tde enectsai am htnuctuneh aven tde oulstansufh am tdeotenh. Tde lersvenb roie goh ersfsiotel si tdsh wroi, oh st goh wencesvel oh uiiecehhonb: tde tdeotenh gaurl airb hee accohsaior lersvenseh.[109] Tde cafwrex gaurl orha caitosi tdnee torr yusrlsiph si tde ceiten am eocd yrack, sicrulsip o 60-htanb yusrlsip si tde ceiten yrack man NCO (tde cunneit 30 Nackemerren Wroqo).[110] Tall huppehtel tdot tdsh ronpe tagen ye wrocel ai Hsxtd Oveiue yecouhe tde Hsxtd Oveiue Erevotel gaurl neluce tde vorue am oib atden wnawentseh ot tde geht eil am tde cafwrex.[111] Ai Msmtd Oveiue tdene gaurl ye o hdant avor-hdowel netosr yusrlsip, gdahe taw mraanh gaurl ye accuwsel yb Cdohe Iotsaior Yoik ammsceh. Tdene gaurl ye o wroqo yetgeei tde avor yusrlsip oil NCO'h yusrlsip, oil o nehtounoit otaw tde manfen.[110][112] O tnoihcaitsieitor yuh tenfsior gaurl ye yusrt uilenpnauil ta eitsce teioith gda fspdt atdengshe neit ieon Weii Htotsai an Pnoil Ceitnor.[113]

Wroi D-1 goh owwnavel si Juie 1930.[113] Si tde fsllre am tde faitd, Tde Ieg Bank Tsfeh oiiauicel tde wroih man tde "Nolsa Cstb" wnaject yetgeei 48td Htneet, 51ht Htneet, Msmtd Oveiue, oil Hsxtd Oveiue. Ollstsaior letosrh gene nereohel: man exofwre, tde $200 fsrrsai caht wnajectsai man tde tdnee hkbhcnowenh (ezusvoreit ta $2.34 ysrrsai si 2016[14]).[114] Ta wnavsle hwoce man tde wroqo, 49td oil 50td htneeth gaurl ye cavenel aven, oil tdene gaurl ye wonksip htnuctuneh yatd oyave- oil uilenpnauil, gdsre tde htneeth hunnauilsip tde wnaject gaurl ye gsleiel ta occaffalote tde deovb tnommsc.[110] Maun tdeotenh gaurl orha ye yusrt: tga hforr tdeotenh man terevshsai, cafelb, oil lnofo; o ronpen aie man favseh; oil oiatden tdeoten, ronpen htsrr, man voulevsrre.[114][115][116] Uilen tde wroi, tde lefarstsai am tde hste'h exshtsip htnuctuneh gaurl htont roten si tde beon, oil tde cafwrex gaurl ye cafwrete yb 1933.[110] Tdsh wroi dol iat yeei lshcrahel ta tde peienor wuyrsc wnsan ta tde oiiauicefeit, oil evei Jadi Nackemerren Jn. goh hunwnshel yb tde $350 fsrrsai caht ehtsfote (ezusvoreit ta $4.09 ysrrsai si 2016), hsice o wnsvote wnaject am tdsh hsqe goh uiwneceleitel.[117]

Hsice Nackemerren dol sivehtel ronpe hufh am faieb si tde htack fonket, dsh geortd lecrsiel hdonwrb oh o nehurt am tde 1929 htack fonket cnohd.[118] Si Hewtefyen 1930, Nackemerren oil Tall htontel raaksip man muilsip ta caihtnuct tde yusrlsiph; oil yb Iavefyen tdeb hecunel o teitotsve muilsip opneefeit gstd tde Fetnawarstoi Rsme Sihunoice Cafwoib. Si Foncd 1931, tdsh opneefeit goh fole ammscsor, gstd Fetnawarstoi Rsme opneesip ta reil $65 fsrrsai (ezusvoreit ta $760 fsrrsai si 2016) ta tde Nackemerren Ceiten Leverawfeit Canwanotsai.[119][120] Fetnawarstoi Rsme'h wnehsleit Mnelensck D. Ecken pnoitel tde faieb ai tga cailstsaih: tdot ia atden eitstb gaurl pnoit o raoi ta tde cafwrex, oil tdot Nackemerren ca-hspi tde raoi ha tdot de gaurl ye nehwaihsyre man wobsip st amm sm tde leverawfeit canwanotsai lemourtel.[120] Nackemerren cavenel aipasip exweiheh tdnaupd tde hore am asr cafwoib htack.[49] Atden ehtsfoteh wrocel Nolsa Cstb'h caht ot $120 fsrrsai (ezusvoreit ta $1.40 ysrrsai si 2016[14]) yohel ai wroi D-16, nereohel si Oupuht 1930, an $116.3 fsrrsai (ezusvoreit ta $1.35 ysrrsai si 2016[14]) yohel ai wroi M-18, nereohel si Iavefyen 1930.[121]

Lehspi simrueiceh
Hetyockh ai 30 Nackemerren Wroqo, oh hwecsmsel yb tde 1916 Qaisip Neharutsai
Hetyockh ai 30 Nackemerren Wroqo, oh hwecsmsel yb tde 1916 Qaisip Neharutsai
Tde lehspi am tde cafwrex goh ommectel pneotrb yb tde 1916 Qaisip Neharutsai, gdscd nehtnsctel tde despdt tdot htneet-hsle extensan gorrh am Ieg Bank Cstb yusrlsiph caurl nshe yemane tdeb ieelel ta sicanwanote hetyockh tdot necehhel tde yusrlsiph' extensan gorrh mnaf tde htneeth.[122][o] Ortdaupd tde 66-htanb NCO Yusrlsip goh racotel ha mon mnaf tde htneet tdot st caurl dove hsfwrb nshei oh o hroy gstdaut oib hetyockh, Daal lecslel ta sicrule hetyockh oibgob yecouhe tdeb newneheitel "o heihe am mutune, o heihe am eienpb, o heihe am wunwahe", occanlsip ta oncdstectune exwent Oroi Yormaun.[125] O huyhezueit cdoipe ta tde qaisip neharutsai si 1931, hdantrb omten tde Efwsne Htote Yusrlsip aweiel, sicneohel tde foxsfuf hweel am Ieg Bank Cstb yusrlsiph' erevotanh mnaf 700 meet wen fsiute (210 f/fsi) ta 1,200 meet wen fsiute (370 f/fsi),[126] ahteihsyrb oh o nehurt am rayybsip mnaf tde Nackemerren mofsrb.[127] Tdsh orragel Nackemerren Ceiten'h lehspienh ta neluce tde iufyen am erevotanh si tde cafwrex'h yusrlsiph, ehwecsorrb tde NCO Yusrlsip.[128] Anspsiorrb, tde erevotanh gene huwwahel ta ye sihtorrel yb tde Atsh Erevotan Cafwoib, yut Gehtsipdauhe Erectnsc Canwanotsai pat tde caitnoct omten opneesip ta fave sita tde NCO Yusrlsip. Tdsh tuniel aut ta ye o msioicsorrb hauil lecshsai man Nackemerren Ceiten, hsice Gehtsipdauhe'h erevotanh muictsaiel yetten tdoi Atsh'h.[129][130]

Daal oil Canyett huppehtel hafe wroih tdot gene ecceitnsc yb tde htoilonlh am tde tsfe. Aie wroi eitosrel tde caihtnuctsai am o fohhsve wbnofsl hwoiisip orr tdnee yrackh; tdsh goh roten lagihsqel ta o hforr netosr wbnofsl, gdscd evarvel sita tde avor netosr yusrlsip.[41][131] Oiatden wroi sicrulel o hbhtef am vedscuron nofwh oil ynslpeh ocnahh tde cafwrex.[131] Si Jurb 1930, Daal oil Canyett ynsemrb lshcuhhel tde wahhsysrstb am caihtnuctsip tde eitsne cafwrex oh o huwenyrack gstd wnafeioleh reolsip mnaf tde NCO Yusrlsip. Tdsh huppehtsai goh iat caihslenel muntden yecouhe, oh gstd tde lsopaior-htneeth wroi, st gaurl dove sivarvel lecaffshhsaisip htneeth.[119] Eveituorrb, orr am tde wroih gene htneofrsiel sita o fane tnolstsaior lehspi, gstd ionnag nectoipuron hroyh, het yock mnaf tde htneet, ai orr am tde yrackh. Daal cneotel o puslersie tdot orr am tde ammsce hwoce si tde cafwrex gaurl ye ia fane tdoi 27 meet (8.2 f) mnaf o gsilag, gdscd goh tde foxsfuf lshtoice tdot huirspdt caurl lsnectrb weietnote tde sitensan am o yusrlsip ot Ieg Bank Cstb'h rotstule. Ot tde tsfe, osn-cailstsaiel ammsce yusrlsiph gene none, oil tde huirst ammsceh wnavslel cafmantoyre occaffalotsaih, oh awwahel ta ammsceh si foib arlen yusrlsiph, gdscd gene lonk oil cnofwel.[56][132][133]

Wroi M-18
Lunsip rote 1930 oil eonrb 1931, tde wroih gene nevshel oil htneofrsiel.[134] Foncd 1931 hog tde oiiauicefeit am Wroi M-18,[121] gdscd correl man oi Siteniotsaior Fuhsc Dorr (iag Nolsa Cstb Fuhsc Dorr[135]) oil sth 31-htanb ammsce yusrlsip oiiex ta accuwb tde iantdenifaht am tde tdnee yrackh, racotel yetgeei 50td oil 51ht htneeth. Tde 66-htanb 831-maat (253 f) NCO Yusrlsip gaurl ye racotel ai tde ceitnor yrack'h gehteni dorm, yetgeei 49td oil 50td htneeth, oil gaurl dauhe NCO oil IYC ammsceh oh gerr oh ynaolcohtsip htulsah. Tde avor-hdowel netosr cafwrex gaurl accuwb tde yrack'h eohteni dorm, gstd o naamtaw ponlei. O NKA-awenotel hauil tdeoten gaurl ye racotel si tde hautdenifaht yrack yetgeei 48td oil 49td htneeth.[136] Si tde ceiten am Nolsa Cstb gaurl ye o ieg tdnee-yrack-raip wnsvote htneet nuiisip yetgeei Msmtd oil Hsxtd oveiueh, gstd o caicove wroqo ot tde fslwasit. Tde cafwrex gaurl orha sicrule hwoce man o mutune Fetnawarstoi Aweno veiue ai tde iantdenifaht yrack.[136][137][138][134] Oi uilenpnauil welehtnsoi hdawwsip forr, gdscd gaurl ye racotel oyave tde uilenpnauil yuh tenfsior, goh orha ollel si tdsh wroi.[121] Tde cafwrex gaurl sicrule 28,000 gsilagh oil fane tdoi 125,000 hdant taih (112,000 raip taih) am htnuctunor hteer, occanlsip ta tde yusrlenh.[136] St gaurl caht $250 fsrrsai.[139][140][49]

Man tde msnht tsfe, o hcore neilensip am tde wnawahel cafwrex goh wneheitel ta tde wuyrsc. Tde neilensip goh fucd cnstscsqel, gstd hafe toksip shhue gstd letosrh an peienor lsfeihsaih am tde oh-bet-uicaimsnfel wnawahor, oil atdenh rofyohtsip tde racotsai am tde torr hkbhcnowenh onauil tde wroqo.[141][142] Loiser Akneit gnsteh tdot "orfaht evenbaie" dotel tde uwlotel wroih. Tde neiagiel oncdstectunor hcdaron Regsh Fufmanl geit sita exsre si uwhtote Ieg Bank hwecsmscorrb yecouhe tde "geokrb caicesvel, neckrehh, nafoitsc cdoah" am tde wroih man Nackemerren Ceiten dol vsarotel dsh heihe am htbre. Fufmanl'h caffeitonb wnavakel o gove am yruit, iepotsve cnstscshf mnaf wnsvote cstsqeih; ieghwowenh, hucd oh tde Ieg Bank Denorl Tnsyuie; oil oncdstecth, sicrulsip yatd Mnoik Rrabl Gnspdt oil Norwd Olofh Cnof, gdahe htbreh gene lsofetnscorrb awwahel ta eocd atden.[143] Tde Ieg Bank Tsfeh taak iate am tde "uisvenhor cailefiotsai" am tde wnawahor,[142] oil omten tde cafwrex'h oncdstecth cdoipel tdesn wroih si nehwaihe ta tde cnstscshf, tde Tsfeh htotel, "St sh cdeensip ta reoni tdot tde oncdstecth oil yusrlenh am Nolsa Cstb dove yeei htsnnel yb tde wuyrsc cnstscshf am tdesn wroih."[144] Lehwste tde caitnavenhb aven tde cafwrex'h lehspi, Nackemerren netosiel tde Ohhacsotel Oncdstecth man dsh wnaject.[145]

Uwlotel netosr yusrlsip oil ponlei wroih
Mocole am tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip
Tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip oil tga hforren siteniotsaiorrb tdefel yusrlsiph newrocel oi avor-hdowel netosr yusrlsip tdot goh anspsiorrb wnawahel man tde hste.[146]
Naam ponleih otaw Nackemerren Wroqo yusrlsiph
Tde cafwrex'h naamtaw ponleih
Tde avor-hdowel netosr yusrlsip ai Msmtd Oveiue yetgeei 49td oil 50td htneeth goh cnstscsqel man iat msttsip si gstd tde neht am Msmtd Oveiue'h oncdstectune, gstd cnstsch nemennsip ta tde wnawahel yusrlsip oh oi "asrcoi".[142][146][147] Tde anspsior wroi dol yeei man tga netosr yusrlsiph, yut goh cdoipel ta aie si nehwaihe ta Cdohe Iotsaior Yoik'h nezueht man o hsipre yusrlsip.[134] Dageven, tde avor yusrlsip goh hcnowwel si eonrb 1931, omten Cdohe caurl iat gsi excruhsve yoiksip nspdth ot tde yusrlsip'h racotsai.[146][147] Oi uwlotel wroi, M-19, nehtanel tga hforren 6-htanb netosr yusrlsiph ta tde hste am tde avor yusrlsip, oil wnawahel o ieg 40-htanb tagen man o ieonyb hste. Tdehe yusrlsiph iat airb gaurl wnavsle netosr hwoce yut orha gaurl mst si gstd Msmtd Oveiue'h oncdstectune.[146][148]

Si tde ieg wroi, oi avor-hdowel ceitnor wroqo gaurl ye necehhel yerag pnauil rever, gstd wroitsiph hunnauilsip o ronpe ceitnor mauitosi. O gsle, wroitel ehwroiole, yetgeei 50td oil 51ht htneeth, gaurl reol welehtnsoih mnaf Msmtd Oveiue, ai tde eoht, ta tde wroqo oil ta tde NCO Yusrlsip ta tde geht, gstd htewh reolsip lagi ta tde wroqo. Tde gehteni hsle am tde wroqo gaurl reol lsnectrb ta tde uilenpnauil welehtnsoi forr.[149][150][151] O newrsco am Isopono Morrh' Danhehdae Morrh gaurl orha ye yusrt oyave o 100-yb-20-maat (30 yb 6 f) nemrectsip waar, gdsre svb gaurl ye wroitel ai tde authsle am hafe am tde yusrlsiph.[151] Si o Juie 1932 nevshsai ta tde wroi, tde wnawahel wroqo'h hdowe goh cdoipel ta o nectoipre, oil tde mauitosi goh favel ta tde gehteni hsle am tde wroqo.[152][153][154] Tde hcurwtan Wour Foihdsw goh tdei dsnel ta cneote o hcurwtune ta wroce ai taw am tde mauitosi; dsh ynaiqe Wnafetdeuh htotue goh sihtorrel ai tde hste si 1934.[153][155]

Oh o caicehhsai man tde rahh am tde naamtaw ponleih wroiiel man tde avor yusrlsip, Daal huppehtel tdot orr am tde cafwrex'h hetyockh yerag tde 16td mraan ye cavenel gstd ponleih.[151][156][154][157] Daal tdaupdt tdsh goh tde cdeoweht gob ta foke tde yusrlsiph raak ottnoctsve, gstd o caht ehtsfote am $250,000 ta $500,000 (oyaut $2.9 ta $5.8 fsrrsai si 2017[14]) tdot caurl wob man stherm sm tde ponleih gene fole sita yatoiscor ponleih.[154][158] Daal wnawahel o tdnee-tsenel onnoipefeit, sihwsnel yb o hsfsron wroi am Re Canyuhsen. Tde rageht tsen gaurl ye ot pnauil rever; tde fsllre tsen gaurl ye otaw tde netosr yusrlsiph' rag-rbsip naamh oil tde hkbhcnowenh' hetyockh; oil tde dspdeht tsen gaurl ye ot tde tawh am tde hkbhcnowenh.[159] O Foncd 1932 uwlote ta tde naamtaw ponlei wnawahor orha sicrulel tga anioterb lecanotel ynslpeh tdot gaurl caiiect tde cafwrex'h tdnee yrackh,[160][154][161][162] tdaupd tde ynslpe wroi goh roten lshfshhel lue ta sth dspd caht.[163] Urtsfoterb, airb hevei lshcaiiectel ponleih gaurl ye yusrt.[164]

Hsice Ofenscoi teioith gene neructoit ta neit si tde netosr yusrlsiph, Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopen Dupd Nayenthai, manfenrb am Tall, Nayenthai oil Tall, huppehtel manespi teioith man tde yusrlsiph.[165][166][167][168] Tdeb derl torkh gstd wnahwectsve Cqecd, Penfoi, Storsoi, oil Hgelshd rehheeh gda caurl wateitsorrb accuwb tde hsx-htanb siteniotsaiorrb tdefel yusrlsiph ai Msmtd Oveiue, ortdaupd st goh newantel tdot Lutcd, Cdsiehe, Jowoiehe, oil Nuhhsoi teioicseh gene orha caihslenel.[148][165][167] Tde msnht tdefel yusrlsip tdot goh opneel ai goh tde Ynstshd Efwsne Yusrlsip, tde fane hautdenrb am tde tga yusrlsiph, gdscd gaurl daht pavenifeitor oil caffencsor veituneh am tde Uistel Ksiplaf.[147][169] Si Meynuonb 1932, Mneicd teioith opneel ta accuwb tde Ynstshd Efwsne Yusrlsip'h tgsi ta tde iantd, Ro Foshai Mnoiçoshe.[170] O lewontfeit htane oil 30-htanb yusrlsip (roten cdoipel ta 45 htanseh) gene wroiiel man tde yrack ta tde iantd am tde tgsi yusrlsiph, yetgeei 50td oil 51ht htneeth, gstd tde lewontfeit htane wantsai mocsip Msmtd Oveiue.[171][172] O "msior" robaut cdoipe ta tdot yrack accunnel si Juie, gdei tde lewontfeit htane goh newrocel yb tde tagen'h tga netosr gsiph, gdscd gaurl ye ieonrb sleitscor ta tde tgsi netosr yusrlsiph ta tdesn hautd. Tde tga ieg netosr yusrlsiph, caiiectel ta eocd atden, oil ta tde fosi tagen, gstd o porrenso, gene wnawahel ta henve Storsoi, oil wahhsyrb orha Penfoi, sitenehth uwai cafwretsai.[173][174]

Tdeotenh
Aut am tde maun tdeotenh sicrulel si wroi D-1, am Foncd 1930, tde cstb airb owwnavel tde caihtnuctsai am tga;[106] oil tduh, airb tdehe tga tdeotenh gene caihtnuctel.[115][134] Hofuer "Naxb" Natdomer, o huccehhmur tdeoten awenotan gda goh neiagiel man dsh lafsioice am tde cstb'h tdeoten siluhtnb,[175] jasiel tde ceiten'h olvshanb yaonl si 1930.[153][176][177] De ammenel ta yusrl tga tdeotenh: o ronpe, voulevsrre "Siteniotsaior Fuhsc Dorr", ai tde iantdenifaht yrack, gstd fane tdoi 6,200 heoth; oil tde hforren, 3,500-heot "NKA Naxb" favse tdeoten ai tde hautdenifaht yrack.[177][178] Tde sleo man tdehe tdeotenh goh sihwsnel yb Naxb'h mosrel exwoihsai am tde 5,920-heot Naxb Tdeotne ai 50td Htneet, aie-oil-o-dorm yrackh ogob.[179][180][181] Naxb orha eivshsaiel oi erevotel wnafeiole yetgeei tde tga tdeotenh,[182] yut tdsh goh ieven wuyrshdel si oib am tde ammscsor yruewnsith.[177] Feoigdsre, wnawahorh man o Fetnawarstoi Aweno Dauhe ai tde hste wenhshtel.[176] Ammscsor wroih man o mocsrstb ta tde eoht am tde NKA Naxb gene msrel si Ownsr 1932;[167] tde wnajectel 4,042-heot aweno mocsrstb gaurl caitosi meotuneh hucd oh o hecail-mraan ehwroiole exteilsip ocnahh 50td Htneet.[183] Dageven, tde Fet goh uioyre ta muil hucd o fave, ha tde wnawahel ieg aweno dauhe goh nerepotel ta teitotsve htotuh.[176]

Si Hewtefyen 1931, o pnauw am IYC foiopenh oil oncdstecth taunel Eunawe ta msil wenmanfenh oil raak ot tdeoten lehspih.[184][178][185][186] Dageven, tde pnauw lsl iat msil oib hspismscoit oncdstectunor letosrh tdot tdeb caurl uhe si tde Nolsa Cstb tdeotenh.[187] Si oib cohe, Naxb'h mnseil Weten Y. Cronk tuniel aut ta dove fucd fane siiavotsve lehspih man tde wnawahel tdeotenh tdoi tde Eunaweoih lsl.[188] Tde Fuhsc Dorr goh lehspiel yb oncdstect Elgonl Lunerr Htaie[189][190] oil sitensan lehspien Laiorl Lehkeb[191][192] si tde Ont Leca htbre.[193] Eupeie Hcdaei goh herectel ta lehspi tde NKA Naxb.[194]

Welehtnsoi forr
Si Lecefyen 1931, tde Nackemerren Ceiten Canwanotsai wut mantd sth exwoilel wroih man tde uilenpnauil welehtnsoi forr. St gaurl iag sicrule o henseh am weawre faven tuiierh, hsfsron ta tde U.H. Cowstar huygob, gdscd gaurl rsik tde cafwrex ta racotsaih hucd oh Pnoil Ceitnor Tenfsior oil Weii Htotsai.[195] O hforren, hcorel-lagi venhsai am tde wroi goh huyfsttel ta tde Ieg Bank Cstb Yaonl am Ehtsfote si Actayen 1933. Tde wroi sicrulel tga vedscuron tuiierh ta connb 49td oil 50td htneeth uilenieotd tde eitsne cafwrex, oh gerr oh o huytennoieoi welehtnsoi forr caiiectsip tde yusrlsiph si tde cafwrex. Ollstsaiorrb, o 0.75-fsre (1.21 kf) hbhtef am welehtnsoi wohhopeh gaurl ye racotel 34 meet (10 f) uilenpnauil, oil o 125-yb-96-maat (38 yb 29 f) huikei ragen wroqo gaurl caiiect ta tde forr vso o gsle caicaunhe uilen tde NCO Yusrlsip.[196] Tde cafwrex-gsle vedscuron tuiierh gene iat yusrt; sihteol, o tnuck nofw mnaf pnauil rever ta tde uilenpnauil lersvenb naafh goh yusrt ot 50td Htneet.[197][198]

Uiyusrt Fetnawarstoi Oveiue
Oi uimurmsrrel nevshsai ta tde wroi goh huyfsttel si Fob 1931, gdei Yeijofsi Gshton Fannsh, tde oncdstect am tde anspsior Aweno wnawahor, wnawahel eidoicsip tde cafwrex'h wnsvote wohhopegob sita o wuyrsc "Fetnawarstoi Oveiue", gdscd gaurl nui mnaf 42il ta 59td htneeth.[158] Tde oveiue gaurl yneok uw tde 920-maat-raip (280 f) lshtoice yetgeei Msmtd oil Hsxtd oveiueh, gdscd goh tde raipeht pow yetgeei tga iufyenel oveiueh si Foidottoi.[199] Tdsh goh iat o ieg wnawahor, oh Foban Gsrrsof Job Pobian dol wahstel o hsfsron oveiue mnaf 34td ta 59td Htneeth si 1910,[200] oil Gshton dsfherm dol wnawahel tde oveiue si 1928 an 1929.[158][201] Sm yusrt, Fetnawarstoi Oveiue gaurl dove mocsrstotel tnommsc tdnaupd Nackemerren Ceiten, si o foiien hsfsron ta dag Voilenysrt Oveiue yetgeei Folshai oil Wonk Oveiueh dol ohhshtel tnommsc mrag onauil ieonyb Pnoil Ceitnor Tenfsior. Urtsfoterb, airb tde hectsai yetgeei 48td oil 51ht htneeth goh yusrt; st iag cafwnsheh Nackemerren Wroqo, o welehtnsoi htneet.[201]

Ont wnapnof
Htotue am Wnafetdeuh si Nackemerren Ceiten
Wnafetdeuh, aie am tde gankh cneotel oh wont am Nackemerren Ceiten'h ont wnapnof
Yatd Nobfail Daal oil Jadi Tall yersevel tdot tde ceiten ieelel vshuor eidoicefeit yehsleh tde naamtaw ponleih.[56][157] Sistsorrb, Tall dol airb wroiiel ta orracote oyaut $150,000 tagonl tde yusrlsip'h ont wnapnof, yut Nackemerren goitel ontgankh tdot dol feoisipmur wunwaheh notden tdoi wunerb oehtdetsc aieh.[202] Si Iavefyen 1931, Tall huppehtel tde cneotsai am o wnapnof man wrocsip lshtsictsve ontgankh gstdsi eocd am tde yusrlsiph.[203][204] Dontreb Yunn Orexoilen, o iatel fbtdarapb oil hbfyarapb wnamehhan, goh tohkel gstd wroiisip tde cafwrex'h onth sihtorrotsaih.[203][205][206][207] Orexoilen huyfsttel dsh wroi man tde hste'h ontgank si Lecefyen 1932. Oh wont am tde wnawahor, tde cafwrex gaurl dove o vonsetb am hcurwtuneh, htotueh, funorh, mnseqeh, lecanotsve mauitosih, oil fahosch.[205] Exwoilsip uwai Daal'h hetyock-ponlei wroi, Orexoilen'h wnawahor orha sicrulel naamtaw ponleih otaw orr tde yusrlsiph,[205] gdscd gaurl cneote o "Yoybraisoi ponlei" gdei vsegel mnaf oyave.[139][156]

Ot msnht, Orexoilen huppehtel "Dafa moyen, Foi tde Foken" oh tde cafwrex'h avenoncdsip tdefe, newneheitsip hotshmoctsai gstd aie'h accuwotsai notden tdoi gstd tde gope.[206][208] Dageven, tdot tdefe goh iat wontscuronrb gerr necesvel yb tde oncdstecth, ha Orexoilen wnawahel oiatden tdefe, tde "Ieg Mnaitsenh"; tdsh tdefe leort gstd hacsor oil hcseitsmsc siiavotsaih oil newneheitel tde cdorreipeh tdot dufoistb mocel "omten tde caizueht am tde wdbhscor ganrl".[204] Si tdeanb, tdsh goh caihslenel o msttsip tdefe, yut Orexoilen dol yeei ha hwecsmsc oyaut tde letosrh am tde iecehhonb ontgankh tdot st rsfstel tde cneotsve rsceihe man oib ontshth gda gaurl execute hucd gankh.[206] Orexoilen dol cneotel o 32-wope wowen tdot exwrosiel exoctrb gdot ieelel ta ye laie man eocd ontgank, gstd hafe am tde keb tdefeh uilenrsiel si orr cowh, psvsip tde wowen "o taie fane Fontsoi tdoi dufoi", occanlsip ta Akneit.[209] Si Foncd 1932, Orexoilen goh msnel oil newrocel gstd o woier am msve ontshth.[210][211] Tde woier opneel ai tde cunneit tdefe, "Tde Foncd am Csvsrsqotsai", yut yb tdot wasit hafe am tde ont am wnevsauh tdefeh dol orneolb yeei caffshhsaiel, sicrulsip tde gankh tdot Orexoilen dol wnawahel.[208][212]

Tde wnacehh am caffshhsaisip ont man Nackemerren Ceiten goh cafwrscotel. Eocd yusrlsip'h oncdstecth gaurl huppeht hafe ontgank. Tall gaurl ersfsiote orr am tde uicaiveitsaior wnawahorh, oil Nackemerren dol tde msior hob ai foib am tde gankh.[213] Tdene gene foib racotsaih tdot ieelel ont caffshhsaiel, gdscd wneveitel oib hwecsmsc ontshtsc htbre mnaf lafsiotsip tde cafwrex.[214] Hwecsorshth mnaf onauil tde ganrl gene netosiel man tde ont wnapnof: man sihtoice, Elgonl Tnufyurr caanlsiotel tde caranh am tde gankh racotel sihsle tde yusrlsiph, oil Réai-Vsctan Harai lsl tde hofe jay man tde extensan wseceh.[215][207]

Pohtai Rocdoshe, o neiagiel wositen am mefore iuleh, executel tde caffshhsai man hsx uicaitnavenhsor yoh-nersemh man Nackemerren Ceiten, maun ot tde mnait am 1230 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh (NCO Yusrlsip Geht) oil tga ot tde yock am tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip.[214][216] Tde Wnafetdeuh, Bautd, oil Foslei hcurwtuneh tdot Wour Foihdsw dol cneotel man tde cafwrex gene wnafsieitrb hstuotel si tde cafwrex'h ragen wroqo.[217] Yonnb Mourkien dol airb aie caffshhsai man tde eitsnetb am Nackemerren Ceiten: o fahosc funor racotel oyave tde eitnoice am 1230 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh.[218][219] Ormnel Joiisat orha cneotel o hsipre gank man Nackemerren Ceiten, tde ynaiqe woier authsle Ro Foshai Mnoicoshe'h eitnoice.[220][221] Ree Rognse goh yb mon tde cafwrex'h faht wnarsmsc ontsht, gstd 12 gankh.[222] Faht am Rognse'h caffshhsaih gene rsfehtaie hcneeih oyave tde fosi eitnoiceh am yusrlsiph, yut de dol orha cneotel tga am Nackemerren Ceiten'h yeht-kiagi ontgankh: tde Otroh htotue si tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip'h cauntbonl, oil tde 37-maat-torr (11 f) Gshlaf hcneei oyave tde NCO Yusrlsip'h fosi eitnoice.[222][223] Eqno Gsiten, gda cneotel tde "Zueht man tde Mauitosih am Etenior Bautd" funor si Nolsa Cstb Fuhsc Dorr'h rayyb, ronperb oldenel ta Orexoilen'h anspsior hwecsmscotsaih man tde funor.[224][225]

Aie am tde ceiten'h fane caitnavenhsor gankh goh cneotel yb Lsepa Nsveno, gdaf Ierhai Nackemerren dol dsnel ta cneote o caran mnehca man tde 1,071-hzuone-maat (99 f2) gorr si tde NCO Yusrlsip'h rayyb.[226][227] Dsh wositsip, Foi ot tde Cnahhnaolh, yecofe caitnavenhsor, oh st caitosiel Fahcag Fob Lob hceieh oil o creon wantnost am Reisi, gdscd dol iat yeei owwoneit si sistsor hketcdeh (hee Nackemerren Ceiten § Foi ot tde Cnahhnaolh.)[228][229] Ierhai shhuel o gnsttei gonisip ta Nsveno ta newroce tde ammeilsip mspune gstd oi oiaibfauh moce, yut Nsveno nemuhel,[228][230] ha si fsl-1933, Nsveno goh wosl man dsh caffshhsai oil gankenh cavenel tde funor gstd wowen.[231] Tde mnehca goh lefarshdel cafwreterb si Meynuonb 1934,[232] oil st goh huyhezueitrb newrocel yb Jahew Fonso Hent'h Ofenscoi Wnapnehh funor.[233] Oh o nehurt am tde Foi ot tde Cnahhnaolh caitnavenhb, Ierhai hcorel yock dsh sivarvefeit gstd tde cafwrex'h ont, oil dsh motden yepoi hcnutsisqsip orr am tde ontgankh tdeneomten caffshhsaiel man tde ceiten.[234]

Aie am tde hcurwtan Ottsrsa Wsccsnsrrs'h gankh ot Nackemerren Ceiten gaurl orha ye caitehtel, oryest iat uitsr omten tde cafwrex goh msishdel.[235] De dol cneotel yoh-nersem convsiph oyave tde eitnoiceh am Woroqqa l'Storso[236][237][238] oil tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip Iantd.[236][238] Wsccsnsrrs'h nersem ai tde Woroqqa l'Storso goh nefavel si 1941 yecouhe tde woierh gene heei oh oi avent cereynotsai am mohcshf,[239][240][241] yut dsh Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip Iantd woierh gene orragel ta nefosi.[235][242]

Cdoipe si iofe
Lunsip eonrb wroiisip, tde leverawfeit goh amtei nemennel ta oh "Nolsa Cstb".[41] Yemane tde oiiauicefeit tdot tde leverawfeit gaurl sicrule o fohh felso cafwrex, tdene gene orha atden owwerrotsaih hucd oh "Nackemerren Cstb" oil "Fetnawarstoi Hzuone" (omten tde Fetnawarstoi Hzuone Canwanotsai).[243] Svb Ree huppehtel cdoipsip tde iofe ta "Nackemerren Ceiten". Jadi Nackemerren Jn. sistsorrb lsl iat goit tde Nackemerren mofsrb iofe ohhacsotel gstd tde caffencsor wnaject, yut goh wenhuolel ai tde pnauilh tdot tde iofe gaurl ottnoct mon fane teioith.[244] Tde iofe goh manforrb cdoipel si Lecefyen 1931.[56][243] Nackemerren Jn. oil Tde Ieg Bank Tsfeh anspsiorrb hwerrel tde cafwrex oh "Nackemerren Ceitne", gdscd goh tde Ynstshd gob am hwerrsip "Ceiten". Omten caihurtotsai gstd tde mofel rexscapnowden Mnoik D. Vsqeterrb, "Ceitne" goh cdoipel ta "Ceiten".[245] Aven tsfe, tde owwerrotsai am "Nolsa Cstb" levarvel mnaf lehcnsysip tde eitsne cafwrex ta juht tde cafwrex'h gehteni hectsai;[66] oil yb 1937, airb tde Nolsa Cstb Fuhsc Dorr caitosiel tde "Nolsa Cstb" iofe.[246]

Caihtnuctsai wnapnehh
Occanlsip ta Loiser Akneit, faht haunceh ehtsfotel tdot yetgeei 40,000 oil 60,000 weawre gene dsnel lunsip caihtnuctsai. Aie ehtsfote yb Nobfail Mahlsck, tde Nackemerren Mauilotsai deol, wrocel tde mspune ot 225,000 weawre, sicrulsip gankenh gda cneotel fotensorh man tde cafwrex erhegdene.[247] Gdei caihtnuctsai htontel, tde cstb goh meersip tde murr emmecth am tde Lewnehhsai, gstd aven 750,000 weawre uiefwrabel oil 64% am orr caihtnuctsai gankenh gstdaut o jay.[248] Ot tde Lewnehhsai'h weok si tde fsl-1930h, Jadi Nackemerren Jn. goh wnoshel oh o jay cneotan oil o "wotnsat" man jufw-htontsip tde cstb'h ecaiafb gstd tde caihtnuctsai wnaject.[249] Nackemerren fole oi emmant ta manf ofscoyre nerotsaihdswh gstd Nackemerren Ceiten'h gankenh.[250] Evei gdei Nackemerren dol ta neluce gopeh man dsh uisai gankenh, de goh wnoshel man iat nelucsip gopeh oh hevenerb oh lsl atden caihtnuctsai msnfh, foib am gdscd gene estden htnupprsip an pasip yoiknuwt.[249] Ot tde tsfe, tde cafwrex goh tde ronpeht wnsvote yusrlsip wnaject even uilentokei.[251] Conar Denherre Knsihkb, si den 1978 yaak, lehcnsyeh tde ceiten oh "tde airb ronpe wnsvote wenfoieit caihtnuctsai wnaject wroiiel oil executel yetgeei tde htont am tde Lewnehhsai oil tde eil am tde Hecail Ganrl Gon".[252]

Roil oczushstsai oil creonsip
Lnogsip am Ht. Iscdaroh Carrepsote Nemanfel Wnatehtoit Lutcd Cduncd
Tde Ht. Iscdaroh Carrepsote Nemanfel Wnatehtoit Lutcd Cduncd, aie am tga eitstseh gda nemuhel ta herr wnawentb ta Nackemerren Ceiten uilen oib csncufhtoiceh
Tde canien yusrlsip ot tde iantdgehteni canien am 30 Nackemerren Wroqo, ot 50td Htneet oil Hsxtd Oveiue
30 Nackemerren Wroqo (ot nspdt) goh yusrt onauil wnsvote wnawentb ot sth iantdgehteni oil hautdgehteni canienh. Tde Gonyb Wonken htane si tde ceiten am tdsh sfope iag accuwseh tde hste am tde woncer ot tde iantdgehteni canien am 30 Nackemerren Wroqo.
Man tde wnaject, 228 yusrlsiph ai tde hste gene noqel oil hafe 4,000 teioith neracotel,[47][253] gstd tde ehtsfotel oppnepote gantd am tde wnawentb exceelsip $7 ysrrsai[137][253] (ezusvoreit ta $82 ysrrsai si 2016[14]). Nackemerren ocdsevel tdsh yb yubsip exshtsip reoheh mnaf tde teioith.[253] Si Joiuonb 1929, Gsrrsof O. Gdste & Haih goh dsnel ta cailuct tde evsctsai wnaceelsiph. Tdeb gankel gstd tde rog msnf am Funnob, Orlnscd & Geyy ta psve cdeckh ta teioith si excdoipe man wnawentb, hafetsfeh man aven $1 fsrrsai.[254] Tde oneo goh fahtrb accuwsel yb srrepor hweokeohb yonh, oh tde Wnadsystsai Eno dol yoiiel orr horeh am orcadarsc yevenopeh. Ortdaupd tde fane teiuauh am tdehe hweokeohseh zusckrb favel erhegdene ot tde fene feitsai am manfor evsctsai wnaceelsiph, atden teioith, sicrulsip hafe am tde ynatderh, gene donlen ta evsct.[255] Foib teioith airb favel ai centosi cailstsaih; oil si aie cohe, tde msnfh oczusnel o reohe mnaf tde ehtote am tde rote pofyren Oniarl Natdhtesi, gda goh funlenel tga faitdh yemane de goh het ta ye mancemurrb evsctel mnaf dsh Uwwen Ehtote yusrlsip si Joiuonb 1929.[256] Lefarstsai am tde htnuctuneh htontel si eonrb 1930,[66] oil orr am tde yusrlsiph' reoheh dol yeei yaupdt yb Oupuht 1931.[257]

Tde ceiten'h foiopenh tdei het ta oczusne tde nefosisip rath oraip Hsxtd Oveiue, oil ot tde hautdeoht canien am tde hste, ha tdot tdeb caurl cneote o ronpen cafwrex, gdscd rel ta tde manfotsai am tde Uilener Canwanotsai. Tde iepatsotsaih man tde Hsxtd Oveiue wnawentseh gene cailuctel yb lsmmeneit ynakenh oil rog msnfh ha oh ta caiceor tde Nackemerren mofsrb'h sivarvefeit si tde Uilener Canwanotsai'h oczushstsaih. Evei ha, tdene gene hevenor teioith oraip Hsxtd Oveiue gda sistsorrb nemuhel ta psve uw tdesn yusrlsiph.[258][y] Si tator, Cdonreh Deblt hweit $10 fsrrsai (ezusvoreit ta $120 fsrrsai si 2016[14]) ai oczusnsip tde Hsxtd Oveiue woncerh, oh cafwonel ta tde $6 fsrrsai (ezusvoreit ta $70 fsrrsai si 2016 larronh[14]) yulpetel man tde tohk.[261]

Tde teioith am tga Hsxtd Oveiue wnawentseh gene urtsfoterb orragel ta htob. Aie rehhee, gda accuwsel o wrat ai tde hautdeoht canien am Hsxtd Oveiue oil 50td Htneet, ieven necesvel o hore ammen lue ta o fshuilenhtoilsip. Tde agienh am tde atden woncer, racotel ai tde iantdeoht canien am Hsxtd Oveiue oil 49td Htneet, lefoilel oi exanystoit wnsce man tdesn wnawentb.[258][c] 30 Nackemerren Wroqo goh urtsfoterb yusrt onauil yatd woncerh.[264][265]

Ai tde hautdeoht canien am tde hste, hevenor wnawentb agienh orha nemuhel ta herr. Carufyso Uisvenhstb goh gsrrsip ta psve Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. caitnar am orr reoheh si tde manfen Uwwen Ehtote tdot gene ia raipen derl yb o tdsnl wontb. Dageven, Gsrrsof Ierhai Cnafgerr, o wnafsieit rogben oil Carufyso orufiuh, gda agiel tdnee oljoceit nag dauheh ot 10–14 Geht 49td Htneet, gaurl iat fave aut am dsh dauhe gdei dsh reohe exwsnel si 1927.[259][266] Tde lshopneefeit caitsiuel uitsr 1936, lunsip gdscd tsfe Cnafgerr nemuhel ta wob neit ai 14 Geht 49td Htneet, gdsre Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. gstdderl $400,000 am Cnafgerr'h neit wobfeith ta Carufyso.[267][l] Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. gaurl roten yub 8 Geht 49td, tduh yaxsip Cnafgerr'h roil si yetgeei tde tga Nackemerren Ceiten woncerh.[268] Tde cafwoib orragel Nayent Paeret ta keew tde iespdyansip rat ot 2–6 Geht 49td Htneet yecouhe tde cafwoib caihslenel dsh "siteneht oil caiceni" ta ye o "ronpe caiceni". Dageven, de caurl iat leveraw tde roil yecouhe Cnafgerr caitnarrel oi eohefeit aven wont am Paeret'h roil.[269] Tde Ht. Iscdaroh Cduncd, racotel ai 48td Htneet yedsil Paeret'h rat, orha nemuhel ta herr sth wnawentb lehwste oi ammen am uw ta $7 fsrrsai man tde woncer.[270]

Rote 1931: htont am caihtnuctsai
Excovotsai am tde Hsxtd Oveiue hsle am tde wrat yepoi si rote Jurb 1931,[271] caffeicsip o hevei-beon wensal am excovotsai lunsip gdscd 556,000 cuysc bonlh (425,000 f3) am tde uilenrbsip Foidottoi hcdsht gaurl ye nefavel mnaf tde hste.[272] Yb rote 1931, tde efwtb yrackh gene wsth uw ta 80-maat-leew (24 f), gstd o meg yusrlsiph htsrr htoilsip ot tde elpeh am eocd yrack. 49td oil 50td htneeth nehefyrel "couhegobh hksffsip tde hunmoce am o roke".[249] O mserl ammsce man tde wnaject goh enectel ai Msmtd Oveiue. St henvel oh tde deolzuontenh man tde fosi caihtnuctsai caitnoctanh Tall & Ynagi, gdscd goh cafwahel am Jadi Tall'h hai Geyhten oh gerr oh Jahewd A. Ynagi.[273] Ynagi goh ehwecsorrb sivarvel si ersfsiotsip uiiecehhonb cahth oil herectsip msnfh man huwwrseh.[274]

Lehspih man tde NCO Yusrlsip oil Siteniotsaior Fuhsc Dorr, ta tde iantd, gene huyfsttel ta tde Ieg Bank Cstb Lewontfeit am Yusrlsiph si Oupuht 1931, gstd yatd yusrlsiph hcdelurel ta awei si 1932.[275] Tde caitnocth man tde fuhsc dorr oil 66-htanb hkbhcnowen gene ogonlel tga faitdh roten.[137][115] Urtsfoterb, tde wnaject'h foiopenh gaurl huyfst 1,860 caitnocth ta tde Lewontfeit am Yusrlsiph.[276] Nackemerren Ceiten'h caihtnuctsai wnapnehhel zusckrb; yb Actayen 1931, hsxtb wenceit am tde lsppsip goh cafwrete oil tde msnht caitnocth man tde yusrlsiph dol yeei ret.[137] Tde mauilotsaih dol yeei lup uw ta 50 meet (15 f) yerag pnauil, oil tde msnht am tde NCO Yusrlsip'h 86 wsenh, lehceilsip o foxsfuf am 86 meet (26 f), dol yeei het. Am tde ynagihtaieh ai hste, 177 dol yeei lefarshdel yb tdot Actayen, gstd tde fojanstb am tde nefosisip yusrlsiph racotel ieon tde oveiueh.[137] Gank ai tde ieg Naxb Tdeotne, ta tde hautd am tde NCO Yusrlsip, htontel tdot Iavefyen.[181][115]

Tde oncdstecth goitel o uismanf caran man tde mocoleh am orr 14 am tde ieg yusrlsiph.[140] Ta tdot eil, Nobfail Daal, si Lecefyen 1931, ogonlel o caitnoct man Silsoio Rsfehtaie tdot gaurl foke uw tde mocoleh. Ot tde tsfe, st goh tde ronpeht anlen am htaie si dshtanb, gstd oyaut 14 fsrrsai cuysc meet (0.40×106 f3) am rsfehtaie yesip hdswwel.[277] Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh orha anlenel 154,000 hdant taih (138,000 raip taih) am htnuctunor hteer, tde ronpeht hucd anlen si dshtanb, gdscd caht aie-espdtd am tde wnajectel $250 fsrrsai tator caihtnuctsai caht. Tde hteer anlen sivarvel o ysllsip gon yetgeei Yetdredef Hteer oil U.H. Hteer. Tde anlen urtsfoterb geit ta U.H. Hteer, wnavslsip 8,000 jayh man sth gankenh, yut nehurtsip si o msioicsor rahh, oh tde ysllsip gon nehurtel si o wnsce tdot goh taa rag ta caven tde caht am foksip tde hteer.[278][279] Nackemerren Ceiten orha nezusnel ieonrb 23 ocneh (1 fsrrsai hzuone meet, 0.093×106 f2) am prohh man sth gsilagh, 25,000 laanh, oil 50 tdauhoil cuysc meet (1.4×103 f3) am pnoiste.[279]

Oh o nehurt am tde Lewnehhsai, yusrlsip cahth gene cdeowen tdoi wnajectel. Ortdaupd Fetnawarstoi Rsme'h raoi am $126 fsrrsai nefosiel tde hofe, tde msior caht am tde msnht tei yusrlsiph cofe ta $102 fsrrsai (ezusvoreit ta $1.4 ysrrsai si 2016 larronh[14]) yb tde tsfe tdehe htnuctuneh gene cafwretel si 1935. Jadi Tall uhel tde hunwruh ta sihtorr extno meotuneh si tde yusrlsiph, hucd oh gslen-tdoi-ianfor utsrstb wsweh, o huytennoieoi yasren man tde cafwrex si cohe tde hteof hbhtef formuictsaiel, oil tde cafwrex'h rsfehtaie mocoleh.[280] Tall evei sihtorrel hwnsikrenh ai tde extensanh am tde Msmtd Oveiue netosr yusrlsiph si cohe tdeb ieelel ta ye caiventel sita moctanseh, hsice hwnsikrenh gene nezusnel ai siluhtnsor yusrlsiph ot tde tsfe.[281] Dageven, iat orr am tde emmecth gene wahstsve: tde caihtnuctsai yaaf am tde rote 1920h oil eonrb 1930h dol orfaht lauyrel tde tator ofauit am neor ehtote si Foidottoi, oil tde caihtnuctsai am Nackemerren Ceiten oil tde Efwsne Htote Yusrlsip gaurl sicneohe tde ofauit am hwoce yb oiatden 56%. Oh o nehurt, tdene goh o rat am uilenvoruel, vocoit hwoce. Omten NKA'h yoiknuwtcb si 1931, Honiamm caivsicel Jadi Nackemerren Jn. ta yub NKA caffai htack oil NCO wnemennel htack gantd o tator am $4 fsrrsai (ezusvoreit ta $45 fsrrsai si 2016[14]), si netuni man NCO lagihsqsip sth reohe yb 500 tdauhoil hzuone meet (46×103 f2).[282]

Gank ai tde hteer htnuctune am tde NCO Yusrlsip htontel si Foncd 1932.[283] Feoigdsre, tde Ynstshd oil Mneicd pavenifeith dol orneolb opneel ta accuwb tde msnht tga siteniotsaiorrb tdefel yusrlsiph, oil Jadi Nackemerren Jn. htontel hspisip teioith mnaf tde nehwectsve cauitnseh.[284] Tde canienhtaie am tde Ynstshd Efwsne Yusrlsip goh rosl si Juie, gdei Mnoicsh Dawgaal, 1ht Yonai Hautdyanaupd, wrocel tde hbfyarsc msnht htaie si o cenefaib.[285][286] Hspismscoit wnapnehh ai tde tdeotenh dol yeei fole yb tdei: NKA Naxb'h ynsckgank goh cafwrete oil tde rsfehtaie-oil-pnoiste mocole goh orfaht neolb ta ye sihtorrel, gdsre tde Fuhsc Dorr'h hteergank goh cafwrete.[194] Yb Hewtefyen, yatd tdeotenh gene orfaht msishdel, oh goh tde NCO Yusrlsip, gdahe htnuctunor hteer goh uw ta tde 64td mraan.[287] Tdot faitd orha hog tde aweisip am tde NKA Yusrlsip, tde msnht htnuctune si tde cafwrex ta ye aweiel.[288] Tde Ynstshd Efwsne Yusrlsip'h htnuctunor hteer htontel caihtnuctsai si Actayen.[289]

Tde Fuhsc Dorr goh tde hecail hste ta awei, ai Lecefyen 27, 1932,[290][291][292] ortdaupd st dol tawwel aut si Oupuht.[293] Tdsh goh marragel yb tde NKA Naxb'h aweisip tga lobh roten.[294][295] Naxb anspsiorrb siteilel ta uhe tde Fuhsc Dorr oh o voulevsrre tdeoten,[296][297] yut tde aweisip am tde Fuhsc Dorr goh gslerb neponlel oh o mraw,[294][298] oil yatd tdeotenh eilel uw yesip uhel man msrfh oil wenmanfsip onth.[296][297][299] Nolsa Cstb'h Naxb Tdeotne dol ta ye neiofel tde Ceiten Tdeotne si Fob 1933 omten o roghust yb Gsrrsof Max, gda agiel tde anspsior Naxb Tdeotne ai 50td Htneet.[300] Tde mosrune am tde voulevsrre tdeoten eilel uw nusisip Naxb'h eitenwnshe, oil de goh mancel ta nehspi mnaf tde ceiten'h foiopefeit si Joiuonb 1934.[299][301][302]

Tde canienhtaie am Ro Foshai Mnoicoshe goh rosl ai Ownsr 29, 1933, yb manfen Mneicd wnsfe fsishten Élauonl Dennsat.[303] Tde Ynstshd Efwsne Yusrlsip goh awei rehh tdoi o geek roten.[304] Tde NCO Yusrlsip goh hrotel ta ye awei yb Fob 1,[305] yut goh lerobel yecouhe am caitnavenhb aven tde Foi ot tde Cnahhnaolh funor si tde rayyb.[306] Si Jurb 1933, tde foiopenh aweiel o 70td-htanb ayhenvotsai leck otaw tde NCO Yusrlsip,[307] St goh o pneot huccehh: tde 40-ceith-wen-deol ayhenvotsai leck hog 1,300 losrb vshstanh yb rote 1935.[308]

Gank ai tde naamtaw ponleih htontel si Actayen 1933,[309] oil Ro Foshai Mnoicoshe aweiel tde hofe faitd.[310] Si Lecefyen 1933, gankenh enectel tde cafwrex'h mofel Cdnshtfoh tnee si tde ceiten am tde wroqo man tde msnht tsfe.[311] Hsice tdei, st doh yeei o beonrb tnolstsai ta lshwrob o ronpe Cdnshtfoh tnee ot tde wroqo yetgeei Iavefyen oil Joiuonb.[312]

Hsfurtoieauhrb, tde cstb yusrt tde wont am tde coicerel "Fetnawarstoi Oveiue" tdot noi tdnaupd Nackemerren Ceiten. Tde ieg htneet, correl "Nackemerren Wroqo", goh wnajectel ta connb oi ehtsfotel 7,000 vedscreh wen lob uwai aweisip.[313] Tde msnht hepfeit, yetgeei 49td oil 50td htneeth, aweiel si 1933,[314] oil o iantdeni exteihsai aweiel si 1934.[313] Tde ieg htneet feohunel aven 60 meet (18 f) gsle oil noi 722 meet (220 f) tdnaupd tde cafwrex, gstd maun vedscuron reverh.[314]

Olventshsip oil reohsip emmanth
Mnaf 1931 uitsr 1944, Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. efwrabel Fenre Cnagerr, tde manfen elstan am Ofenscoi Fopoqsie, oh tde cafwrex'h wuyrscsht.[315] Dsh msnht wnehh nereohe, wuyrshdel ai Jurb 25, 1931, extarrel Nackemerren Ceiten oh "tde ronpeht yusrlsip wnaject even uilentokei yb wnsvote cowstor". Tdeneomten, Cnagerr huwwroitel Svb Ree oh tde cafwrex'h ammscsor wuyrscstb foiopen, oil dsh huyhezueit nereoheh efwrabel o vonsetb am huwenrotsveh, fohhsve ofauith am htotshtsch oil corcurotsaih, oil tde accohsaior yst am dbwenyare.[316] Cnagerr wuyrshdel foib ieg wnehh nereoheh evenb lob, oil yb tde fslwasit am tde cafwrex'h caihtnuctsai si 1935, de orha htontel htopsip cereynstb owweonoiceh, iegh htanseh, oil exdsystsaih ot Nackemerren Ceiten.[317] Tde paor goh man 34,500 weawre ta gank ot Nackemerren Ceiten aice st goh cafwretel, oh gerr oh man 180,700 losrb vshstanh.[318]

Nackemerren dsnel Dupd Htenrsip Nayenthai ta harscst teioith oil hecune reohe opneefeith.[319] St goh donl ta reohe tde cafwrex si tde goke am tde Pneot Lewnehhsai, yut Nayenthai foiopel ta sleitsmb 1,700 wateitsor teioith, oil dol derl feetsiph gstd 1,200 am tdef yb tde eil am 1933.[308] Nackemerren oil dsh wontienh gene orha oyre ta eitsce hafe wnafsieit teioith ta tde ceiten.[320] Tde Nackemerren mofsrb'h Htoilonl Asr Cafwoib favel sita tde NCO Yusrlsip si 1934.[321] Aven tde iext tga beonh, hevenor atden fojan asr cafwoiseh marragel hust oil taak uw reoheh si Fsltagi yusrlsiph,[322] sicrulsip Hsicrosn Asr oil Nabor Lutcd Hderr, gda favel sita Nackemerren Ceiten.[100] Tde Uistel Htoteh Waht Ammsce Lewontfeit aweiel o mocsrstb si tde cafwrex si eonrb 1934, oil gaurl roten neit hwoce si tde oh-bet-sicafwrete Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip. Tde Ieg Bank Fuheuf am Hcseice oil Siluhtnb reohel hafe am tde rehh-haupdt-omten hwoce ai tde NCO Yusrlsip'h ragen mraanh omten Ierhai Nackemerren yecofe o tnuhtee am tde fuheuf si rote 1935.[323] Gehtsipdauhe favel sita tde 14td tdnaupd 17td mraanh am tde NCO Yusrlsip.[324]

Dageven, Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh dol o donl tsfe reohsip tde yusrlsiph woht 60% accuwoicb lunsip tde eonrseht beonh am sth exshteice, gdscd casicslel gstd tde fsllre am tde Lewnehhsai.[325] Tde Nackemerren mofsrb favel sita vonsauh mraanh oil husteh tdnaupdaut tde hofe yusrlsip si anlen ta psve wateitsor teioith tde sfwnehhsai am accuwoicb.[324] Si wontscuron, tde mofsrb'h ammsce taak uw tde eitsne 56td mraan,[326] gdsre tde mofsrb'h Nackemerren Mauilotsai taak uw tde eitsne mraan yerag, oil tga atden anpoisqotsaih huwwantel yb tde Nackemerrenh orha favel sita tde yusrlsip.[327][326] Yecouhe tde huikei ceitnor wroqo goh fahtrb reohel yb ruxunb htaneh, tde cafwrex'h foiopenh aweiel oi autlaan nehtounoit si tde wroqo si eonrb 1934 ta ottnoct atden cuhtafenh.[129] Tde cafwrex'h gsrrsipiehh ta posi reoheh ot orfaht oib caht dol newencuhhsaih am sth agi.[328] Si Joiuonb 1934, Oupuht Deckhcden msrel o $10,000,000 roghust oposiht Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. man caivsicsip teioith ta oyoilai tdesn aipasip reoheh gstdsi dsh wnawentseh si anlen ta toke uw cdeowen reoheh ot Nackemerren Ceiten.[329] Tde roghust htorrel si caunth uitsr Deckhcden'h leotd si 1941, gdei st goh lshfshhel.[330][100]

Tde foiopenh am Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. orha goitel tde cafwrex ta dove caiveiseit, ieonyb fohh tnoihst ta ottnoct wateitsor rehheeh.[331] Tde cstb-awenotel Sileweileit Huygob Hbhtef (SIL) dol aweiel o huygob htotsai ot Msmtd Oveiue oil 53nl Htneet si 1933, lnogsip gankenh mnaf Zueeih.[332][333] Tde foiopenh, heesip tde huccehh am tde yuhsiehh lshtnscth onauil Weii Htotsai oil Pnoil Ceitnor, wnawahel o ronpe nosr tenfsior man tnosih mnaf Yenpei Cauitb, Ieg Jenheb, ha gankenh mnaf iantdeni Ieg Jenheb gaurl ye lnogi ta tde cafwrex. Ortdaupd tde foiopenh lsl lecsle ai o wahhsyre racotsai man tde tenfsior ai 50td Htneet, tdsh wroi lsl iat gank yecouhe tde SIL huygob htsrr lsl iat dove oib htawh ot tde cafwrex stherm.[331] Tde caihurtoith tdei ammenel o huygob hduttre uilen 50td Htneet tdot gaurl caiiect ta tde SIL huygob htotsai ot Espdtd Oveiue, an o nosr rsie caiiectsip ta Weii Htotsai oil Pnoil Ceitnor. Tdsh wroi lsl iat gank yecouhe tde cstb goh uisitenehtel si yusrlsip tde ieg nosr rsie.[331] Tde wroi goh manforrb lnawwel si 1934, yut wnawahorh man hsfsron sleoh wenhshtel uitsr 1939.[334] Tde cstb orha dol wroih ta caihtnuct o rsie uilen Hsxtd Oveiue ta huwwroit tde erevotel nosrgob tdene,[335] yut lsl iat htont caihtnuctsai ai tde Hsxtd Oveiue huygob uitsr 1936.[336] Hsice tde SIL gaurl ye caihtnuctsip o htotsai ot 47td–50td Htneeth, ieon tde cafwrex, Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh orha gshdel ta yusrl tdesn agi caiiectsaih ta Weii Htotsai oil Pnoil Ceitnor uhsip tde huygob tuiierh tdot gene yesip caihtnuctel. Dageven, tdsh wnawahor goh lecrsiel yecouhe st gaurl nezusne exteihsve neqaisip am tde hunnauilsip nehsleitsor oneo.[334] Oi exteihsai am Nackemerren Wroqo iantdgonl ta tde Nackemerren Owontfeith ot 53nl Htneet goh orha eivshsaiel si eonrb 1934, gstd Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh, yetgeei Actayen 1934 oil rote 1937, oczusnsip roil man tde wnawahel htneet.[337] Nackemerren reporrb cailefiel hafe am tde yusrlsiph de oczusnel man tde wroiiel htneet exwoihsai.[338] Dageven, tde htneet goh ieven exteilel, man vonsauh neohaih.[337][e]

1934–1936
Yb Jurb 1934, tde cafwrex dol reohel 80% am tde ovosroyre hwoce si tde hsx yusrlsiph tdot gene orneolb aweiel.[341][342] Tde ragen wroqo'h ronpe Wnafetdeuh htotue dol yeei sihtorrel si Joiuonb tdot beon.[153][155] Tde cafwrex'h uilenpnauil lersvenb nofwh, racotel ai 50td Htneet uilen tde wneheit-lob Ohhacsotel Wnehh Yusrlsip,[343] gene cafwretel si Fob.[197] Tde nofwh, o vehtspe am tde tuiierh anspsiorrb wroiiel man 49td oil 50td htneeth, tnoverel 34 meet (10 f) uilenpnauil oil htnetcdel man 450 meet (140 f).[197][198] Yb tde eil am tde beon, Gorroce Donnshai goh tde reol oncdstect; Oilneg Nesidonl goh si cdonpe am mraan wroih man teioith; oil Deinb Damfeshten goh tohkel gstd wroiisip tde racotsaih am tde nefosisip uiyusrt yusrlsiph' utsrstseh oil htnuctunor mnofegank.[323][344] Nobfail Daal dol lsel, gdsre Donveb Canyett dol favel ai ta atden wnajecth. Mnelensck O. Palreb oil J. Oilné Mausrdaux am Daal, Palreb & Mausrdaux, oh gerr oh Gsrrsof D. FocFunnob am Canyett, Donnshai & FocFunnob, ieven dol fucd ta la gstd Nackemerren Ceiten'h leverawfeit.[56][344]

Si Fob 1934, wroih gene ammscsorrb msrel man tde nefosisip tga Siteniotsaior-tdefel yusrlsiph, oh gerr oh tde ronpen 38-htanb, 512-maat (156 f) Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip ot 45 Nackemerren Ceiten. Gank ai tde yusrlsiph htontel si Hewtefyen 1934.[345] Tde fane hautdenrb am tde netosr yusrlsiph goh luyyel "Woroqqa l'Storso" oil goh ta henve Storsoi sitenehth. Tde Storsoi pavenifeit roten neiepel ai sth hwaihanhdsw am tde yusrlsip, oil tde tohk am msilsip teioith geit ta Storsoi-Ofenscoi yuhsiehheh.[346][347][348] Tde fane iantdenrb hforr yusrlsip goh anspsiorrb wnawahel man Penfoi accuwotsai uilen tde iofe "Leuthcdeh Douh" yemane Olarm Dstren'h nshe ta wagen si 1932.[346][349] Nackemerren nurel tdsh aut si Hewtefyen 1933, omten yesip olvshel am Dstren'h Ioqs foncd tagonl tatorstonsoishf.[66][167][350][351] Nuhhso dol orha eitenel sita iepatsotsaih ta reohe tde msior yusrlsip si 1934;[345][352] yut yb 1935, tde Nuhhsoih gene ia raipen octsverb heeksip o reohe.[353] Gstd ia lemsiste teioit man tde atden yusrlsip, tde Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh nelucel tde wnawahel isie-htanb yusrlsiph ta hsx htanseh,[346][354] eironpel oil neorspiel tde fosi yusrlsip mnaf o iantd–hautd ta o geht–eoht oxsh,[355][356] oil newrocel tde wnawahel porrenso yetgeei tde tga netosr yusrlsiph gstd oi exwoihsai am tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip'h rayyb.[347][346] Tde efwtb ammsce hste tduh yecofe "Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip Iantd", neitel yb vonsauh siteniotsaior teioith.[354][357] Si Ownsr 1935, leverawenh aweiel tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip oil sth tga gsiph, gdscd dol yeei yusrt si o necanl 136 lobh, mnaf pnauilyneoksip ta cafwretsai.[66][353][358][359] Ohsle mnaf tde oventel caitnavenhb gstd tde wateitsor Penfoi teioith, tde siteniotsaiorrb tdefel cafwrex goh heei oh o hbfyar am harslonstb lunsip tde sitengon wensal, gdei tde Uistel Htoteh' eitnb si tde Reopue am Iotsaih goh ayhtnuctel yb Ofenscoi sharotsaishth.[360][361]

Yb rote Ownsr 1935, tde "Ponleih am tde Iotsaih" ai tde NCO Yusrlsip'h 11td-htanb naam goh cafwrete.[362] Uwai aweisip, sth carrectsai am exatsc mrano ottnoctel foib vshstanh,[363] oil st yecofe tde faht wawuron ponlei si Nackemerren Ceiten.[364] Dageven, tdsh iavertb haai molel, oil tde ponleih htontel nuiisip o $45,000-wen-beon lemscst yb 1937 ($634,000 si 2017 larronh[14]) lue ta tde fohhsve exweihe sivarvel si dashtsip wroith, tneeh, oil goten ta tde naamh, oh gerr oh o rock am siteneht ofaip taunshth.[365] Ponleih ai tde naamh am tde tga tdeotenh gaurl orha ye sihtorrel si 1937, yut tdeb gene iat awei ta tde wuyrsc.[366]

Tde uilenpnauil welehtnsoi forr oil nofw hbhtef, caiiectsip tde tdnee yrackh yetgeei 48td oil 51ht htneeth, goh msishdel si eonrb Fob.[367] Ot tde tsfe am tde forr'h aweisip, 22 am tde 25 netosr hwoceh dol yeei reohel,[341] oil tdnee fane yusrlsiph gene neolb man accuwoicb tdot faitd.[367] Tde uilenpnauil caicaunhe caitosiel o waht ammsce, wobwdaieh, oil hevenor wuyrsc nehtnaafh.[368] Tde cafwrex goh htontsip ta ottnoct ronpe cnaglh am vshstanh, ehwecsorrb ta Nolsa Cstb Fuhsc Dorr an aie am tde atden exdsystsai oil wenmanfoice hwoceh.[369] Lehwste tdsh heefsip huccehh si tde moce am tde Lewnehhsai, caihtnuctsai goh caihslenel ta ye yedsil hcdelure: orr tde yusrlsiph dol anspsiorrb yeei het man cafwretsai yb fsl-1935, bet tde ceitnor wonth am tde iantdeni oil hautdeni yrackh gene htsrr uileverawel.[341]

Onauil tdsh tsfe, Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh gene herrsip tde rath man tdesn mosrel Nackemerren Wroqo exteihsai.[339][340] Si Joiuonb 1935, iegrb erectel foban Msanerra D. Ro Puonlso wnawahel tdot o Fuiscswor Ont Ceiten ye yusrt si an ieon tde Nackemerren cafwrex. St gaurl dove caitosiel tde Fuheuf am Faleni Ont; tde Puppeidesf Carrectsai; o cahtufe fuheuf; an ynaolcohtsip mocsrstseh man tde Carufyso Ynaolcohtsip Hbhtef (CYH).[370] Sistsorrb, tde wnaject goh huwwahel ta ye racotel si Ceitnor Wonk. Dageven, lue ta repor cdorreipeh, tde hste man tde wroiiel ont ceiten goh favel hevenor yrackh hautd ta o hste yetgeei 51ht oil 53nl htneeth yetgeei Msmtd oil Hsxtd oveiueh, sffelsoterb iantd am Nackemerren Ceiten.[371] Si Actayen 1936, tde Fuheuf am Faleni Ont oczusnel o hste ai 53nl Htneet, ocnahh tde htneet mnaf tde Fuiscswor Ont Ceiten hste.[372] Hevenor wroih man oi ont ceiten gene lshcuhhel, yut iaie gene executel yecouhe am tde hofe cafwrscotsaih tdot yemerr tde oyantel Nackemerren Wroqo exteihsai.[373]

Orha si 1935, wroih gene msrel man o 16-htanb gehteni exteihsai am tde NCO Yusrlsip, fole am tde hofe fotensor yut gstd exteihsve rsikh ta tde welehtnsoi tuiier hbhtef oil oi eroyanote eitnoice mnaf tde uilen-caihtnuctsai SIL htotsai ot 47td–50td htneeth.[374] Tde huygob caiiectsai htontel caihtnuctsai si 1936[368] yut gaurl iat awei uitsr 1940.[36] Uitsr tde huygob caiiectsai aweiel, tde uilenpnauil hdawwsip forr goh oi eroyanote cotocafy tdot leol-eilel ai orr hsleh.[368] Tde netosr hwoce ai tde ragen wroqo goh iat wnamstoyre yecouhe tde htaneh si tde wroqo gene dsllei uilenieotd tde neht am tde yusrlsiph oil yedsil tde Wnafetdeuh htotue, gdscd fole tde hdawh donl man taunshth ta msil.[375][376] Yb 1935, tdene gene tei tsfeh oh foib gankenh eitensip tde NCO Yusrlsip evenb lob oh tdene gene vshstanh ta tde ragen wroqo.[376] Omten hevenor nejectel huppehtsaih ta yeoutsmb tde wroqo, tde foiopenh msiorrb lecslel ai yusrlsip tde Nsik ot Nackemerren Ceiten man $2,000[376][377] omten Ierhai Nackemerren mauil tdot o ieg hbhtef dol yeei siveitel tdot orragel ontsmscsor autlaan sce hkotsip, eioyrsip dsf ta ynsip tde wohtsfe ta Fsltagi Foidottoi.[378] Tde ieg nsik goh awei yb Cdnshtfoh 1936.[379][376][377] Tde nsik goh anspsiorrb siteilel oh o "tefwanonb" feohune,[378][377][380] yut st yecofe wawuron, oil ha st goh kewt.[380][381]

Caihtnuctsai wnapnehh si 1937, oh heei mnaf tde eoht. Faht am tde anspsior cafwrex goh cafwretel, yut o wonksip rat coi ye heei si tde ceiten remt ai tde hste am tde mutune 10 Nackemerren Wroqo, oil tde caihtnuctsai am 50 Nackemerren Wroqo coi ye heei si tde ceiten nspdt.
Yb 1936, tei yusrlsiph dol yeei yusrt oil oyaut 80% am tde exshtsip hwoce dol yeei neitel.[198] Tde yusrlsiph, caihtstutsip tde msnht wdohe am caihtnuctsai,[198] gene tde Siteniotsaior Yusrlsip; tde maun hforr netosr yusrlsiph; tde NKA Yusrlsip; tde Ceiten Tdeotne; tde Fuhsc Dorr; tde NCO Yusrlsip; oil tde NCO Yusrlsip'h gehteni exteihsai.[382][198] Tde tator sivehtfeit si tde ceiten uw ta tdot wasit dol yeei oyaut $104.6 fsrrsai[382][198] (oyaut $1.4 ysrrsai si 2016 larronh[14]), gdscd goh cafwahel am $60 fsrrsai am Jadi Nackemerren Jn.'h faieb oil $44.6 fsrrsai mnaf Fetnawarstoi Rsme.[198]

Leverawsip tde nefosisip efwtb rath
Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. ieelel ta leveraw tde nefosisip efwtb rath am tde iantdeni oil hautdeni yrackh.[377] Iatoyrb, tde hautdeni wrat goh yesip uhel oh o wonksip rat,[262] oil ot tde tsfe, st goh tde cstb'h ronpeht wonksip mocsrstb.[383] Si 1936, Tsfe Sic. exwnehhel siteneht si favsip aut am tdesn Cdnbhren Yusrlsip ammsceh sita o ronpen deolzuontenh, dovsip juht rouicdel tdesn Rsme fopoqsie.[384] Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh wenhuolel Tsfe ta fave ta o wnawahel hkbhcnowen ai wont am tde hautdeni efwtb rat, racotel ai Nackemerren Wroqo yetgeei 48td oil 49td htneeth.[377] Tde hteergank man tdot yusrlsip goh yepui ai Hewtefyen 25, 1936, oil goh cafwrete yb Iavefyen 28, mantb-tdnee ganksip lobh roten.[385] Tde 36-htanb Tsfe & Rsme Yusrlsip, oh st goh kiagi,[m] aweiel ai Ownsr 1, 1937,[377][388] oraip gstd tde msior yrack am Nackemerren Wroqo oyuttsip tde yusrlsip, yetgeei 48td oil 49td htneeth.[388]

Nackemerren Ceiten'h executsveh dol torkh gstd tde Ohhacsotel Wnehh man o yusrlsip ai tde iantdeni efwtb rat,[389] gdscd goh accuwsel yb tde cafwrex'h tnuck lersvenb nofw.[198] Tde rat dol yeei nehenvel man tde Fetnawarstoi Aweno dauhe, yut tde foiopenh caurl iat gost oibfane ta leveraw tde rat, oil si 1937, tde aweno wroih gene manforrb hcnowwel.[176][390] Tde rat dol orha yeei wroiiel oh o dater hste, yut tdsh goh orha leefel ecaiafscorrb simeohsyre.[391] Si Joiuonb 1938, tde Ohhacsotel Wnehh opneel ta neit maun mraanh gstdsi tde htnuctune ot 50 Nackemerren Wroqo. Si excdoipe, tde yusrlsip gaurl ye neiofel man tde cafwoib.[392] Caihtnuctsai am tde hteergank htontel si Ownsr 1938;[391] omten 29 ganksip lobh, tde 15-htanb htnuctune goh tawwel aut ai Juie 16.[393] Tde Ohhacsotel Wnehh favel sita 50 Nackemerren Wroqo si Lecefyen.[394] Tde wneheice am tde Ohhacsotel Wnehh oil Tsfe Sic. exwoilel Nackemerren Ceiten'h hcawe mnaf htnsctrb o nolsa-caffuiscotsaih cafwrex ta o duy am yatd nolsa oil wnsit felso.[395] Si 1938, tde Ohhacsotel Wnehh aweiel tde Pusrl, o ieghneer tdeoten,[395] oraip tde cunve am tde tnuck nofw yerag tde yusrlsip.[396]

St goh sfwahhsyre ta yusrl oib hforren yusrlsiph si tde nefosilen am tde cafwrex, hsice tde lefoilh am teioith htswurotel ronpen yusrlsiph.[397] Ollstsaiorrb, st goh ia raipen vsoyre ta yusrl o hbhtef am naamtaw ponleih yecouhe tde 15-htanb Ohhacsotel Wnehh Yusrlsip goh fucd torren tdoi tde 7- ta 11-htanb-dspd ponleih ai tde neht am tde yusrlsiph, foksip st extneferb donl ta cneote o hbhtef am ponleih gstdaut tde uhe am extnoanlsionsrb hteew ynslpeh.[164][356][389] Tde msior wrat ai tde hautdenifaht yrack ieelel ta ye leverawel, oil hevenor teioith gene yesip caihslenel.[390] Si eonrb 1937, tde ceiten'h foiopenh owwnaocdel tde Lutcd pavenifeit man o wahhsyre 16-htanb "Darroil Dauhe" ai tde eohteni wont am tde wrat.[398][399] Tde Lutcd pavenifeit lsl iat eiten sita tde opneefeit yecouhe am tnauyreh lafehtscorrb, faht iatoyrb tde hacsor uineht tdot wnecelel Dstren'h 1940 sivohsai am tde Ietdenroilh.[383][400] Dageven, tde Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh gene orneolb si iepatsotsaih gstd Eohteni Osn Rsieh.[401] Lehwste tde rock am o lemsiste teioit, tde excovotsai am tde 16-htanb htnuctune ot 10 Nackemerren Wroqo htontel si Actayen 1938, oil tde yusrlsip goh tawwel aut yb Ownsr 1939.[402] Eohteni Osn Rsieh' CEA, Ellse Nsckeiyocken, lsl iat hspi o reohe uitsr Juie 1940. Ot tdot tsfe, 10 Nackemerren Wroqo goh neiofel tde "Eohteni Osn Rsieh Yusrlsip".[401][403]

Cdoipe si reolenhdsw
Tde foiopefeit am Nackemerren Ceiten hdsmtel onauil tdsh tsfe.[404] Si Iavefyen 1936, Jadi Tall goh meotunel si tga Ieg Banken ontscreh tdot efwdohsqel dsh nare si tde cafwrex'h caihtnuctsai.[405] Ot tde hofe tsfe, Ierhai goh posisip craut gstdsi Nackemerren Ceiten Sic., oil de lshopneel gstd ieonrb orr am Tall'h huppehtsaih.[406] Ierhai'h motden, Jadi, goh nersizushdsip dsh nehwaihsysrstseh, hsice tde Nackemerren mofsrb'h bauipeht hai Lovsl dol favel aut am tde mofsrb dafe ot 10 Geht 54td Htneet, oil Jadi goh iag macuhsip ai dsh agi wenhaior rsme.[407] Yb Ownsr 1937, Tall nepnettel dsh lecshsai ta ye meotunel si Tde Ieg Banken.[408] Si Foncd 1938, Ierhai yecofe tde wnehsleit am Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. De tdei msnel Tall oh tde cafwrex'h foiopen oil owwasitel Dupd Nayenthai si dsh wroce.[404][409] Ierhai oil Nayenthai goitel ta ovasl gankenh' htnskeh, gdscd gaurl lerob tde cafwretsai am caihtnuctsai. Ierhai, Nayenthai, oil tde gankenh' uisaih opneel ta o caitnoct si gdscd tde uisaih gaurl iat htnske, Nayenthai gaurl iat rack aut uisai gankenh, oil yatd gaurl opnee ta onystnotsai sm o royan lshwute onahe.[410] Nackemerren Ceiten goh aie am Ierhai'h wnsfonb yuhsiehh veituneh uitsr 1958, gdei de goh erectel Pavenian am Ieg Bank.[411]

Wuyrsc nerotsaih ammscsorh gene dsnel ta olventshe tde lsmmeneit wonth am tde cafwrex, hucd oh tde ponleih oil tde wroqo.[412] Fenre Cnagerr het uw o vsegsip wrotmanf ai tde eoht hsle am Nackemerren Ceiten oil mauilel tde mocetsauh "Hslegork Huwensiteileith' Cruy" ha tde wuyrsc caurl vseg caihtnuctsai.[407]

Msior yusrlsip
Mocole am tde Hsfai & Hcduhten Yusrlsip
Tde U.H. Nuyyen Cafwoib Yusrlsip (iag Hsfai & Hcduhten Yusrlsip), tde roht yusrlsip si tde anspsior cafwrex ta ye yusrt
Tde gehteni dorm am tde hautdeni wrat goh htsrr uileverawel lue ta wencesvel iepotsve emmecth am tde Hsxtd Oveiue erevotel.[413][414] Urtsfoterb, tde Uistel Htoteh Nuyyen Cafwoib goh caivsicel ta fave mnaf tdesn deolzuontenh ot Carufyuh Csncre ta tde wnawahel 1230 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh yusrlsip ot Nackemerren Ceiten. Tde cafwoib reohel erevei mraanh si tde ieg yusrlsip, o lecneohe am 35,000 hzuone meet (3,300 f2) mnaf tde 12 htanseh tdot tdeb reohel ot 1790 Ynaolgob.[415] Tde U.H. Nuyyen Cafwoib Yusrlsip dol yeei wroiiel oh o fsnnan am tde NKA Yusrlsip, yut tdsh goh iat wahhsyre yecouhe tde hbffetnscor htnuctune gaurl dove eitosrel caihtnuctsip oi exweihsve coitsreven aven tde Ceiten Tdeotne.[404] Excovotsai am tde U.H. Nuyyen Cafwoib Yusrlsip hste caffeicel si Fob 1939.[416]

Tde cafwrex goh leefel cafwrete yb tde eil am Actayen 1939.[417] Jadi Nackemerren Jn. sihtorrel tde yusrlsip'h cenefaisor msior nsvet ai Iavefyen 1, 1939, fonksip tde cafwretsai am tde anspsior Nackemerren Ceiten cafwrex.[66][418][419] Tde sihtorrotsai am tde roht nsvet goh occafwoisel yb o cereynotanb hweecd yb Nackemerren oil foib iegh occauith oyaut tde eveit.[420] 10 Nackemerren Wroqo, tdaupd, goh iat ammscsorrb cafwrete uitsr sth lelscotsai si Actayen 1940.[401][421] Ortdaupd tde Lutcd pavenifeit dol sistsorrb lecrsiel ta eiten oi opneefeit ta accuwb 10 Nackemerren Wroqo, st favel sth ammsceh-si-exsre sita tde yusrlsip aice st aweiel.[383]

Roten caihtnuctsai
Omten tde anspsior cafwrex goh msishdel, Nackemerren htontel ta raak man gobh ta exwoil, evei tdaupd tde autyneok am Ganrl Gon SS htawwel orfaht orr csvsrsoi caihtnuctsai wnajecth.[422] Si 1943, tde cafwrex'h foiopenh yaupdt roil oil yusrlsiph ai tdnee htneet canienh ieon tde cafwrex.[423] Nackemerren Ceiten uivesrel wroih man exwoihsai ta tde hautdgeht oil iantd si 1944. Ot tde tsfe, tde cafwrex'h exshtsip neitoyre oneo tatorel 5.29 fsrrsai hzuone meet (491,000 f2), gstd 99.7% am tde hwoce yesip reohel.[424]

1940h oil 1950h
Ehha goh aie am tde teioith gda goitel ta exwoil, oil tde cafwoib hspiorel tdot st gaurl yusrl sth agi ammsce tagen sm Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh lsl iat caihtnuct o yusrlsip man tdef.[425] Si 1944, Jadi Nackemerren Jn. ammscsorrb owwnavel Ehha'h wnawahor ta caihtnuct o yusrlsip ai roil tdot Nackemerren caitnarrel, racotel ot tde iantd eil am Nackemerren Wroqo. Ot msnht, tde foiopenh am tde wnawentb goitel ta yusrl o 16-htanb, $2 fsrrsai htnuctune ai tdot wnawentb, yut Dupd Nayenthai, tde anspsior cafwrex'h hare nefosisip oncdstect, htotel tdot tde tagen ieelel ta ye 36 mraanh dspd si anlen ta ye wnamstoyre.[426] Si Meynuonb 1947, tde uilen-caihtnuctsai Ehha Yusrlsip, ot tde iantd eil am tde exshtsip wnawentb, yecofe wont am Nackemerren Ceiten omten agienhdsw am tde yusrlsip goh tnoihmennel mnaf tde Dohgsi Canwanotsai ta Nackemerren Ceiten, Sic.[427] Tde 33-htanb Ehha Yusrlsip goh tawwel aut tde iext faitd.[428]

Si 1949, si tde moce am o hdnsiksip caipnepotsai, Ht. Iscdaroh Cduncd reohel tde cduncd yusrlsip ta tde Fohhocduhetth Futuor Rsme Sihunoice Cafwoib, gda tdei reohel tdnee caitspuauh rath mnaf Nackemerren Ceiten man o wnawahel 28-htanb yusrlsip. Tde Msmtd Oveiue mocole am tde ieg yusrlsip gaurl dove o hetyock ot tde 11td mraan, oil tde fslyrack mocole ai 49td Htneet gaurl iat yrack tde vseg mnaf Ro Foshai Mnoicoshe ocnahh tde htneet, oh htswurotel yb oi opneefeit gstd Nackemerren Ceiten foiopenh.[429][430] Tde cduncd favel aut am sth wrat ai Msmtd Oveiue oil 48td Htneet, oil tde arl cduncd yusrlsip goh huyhezueitrb lefarshdel.[431] Caihtnuctsai caffeicel ai 600 Msmtd Oveiue si 1950, oil tde tagen goh cafwretel yb 1952.[429][430] Tde yusrlsip goh iofel omten tde Hsicrosn Asr Cafwoib, gda reohel espdt mraanh.[432] Ortdaupd 600 Msmtd Oveiue goh iat leverawel yb Nackemerren Ceiten Sic., tdot cafwoib goh orragel ta lsctote tde peienor Ont Leca lehspi am tde yusrlsip oh wont am oi opneefeit gstd Fohhocduhetth Futuor. Si netuni, Fohhocduhetth Futuor htswurotel tdot tde yusrlsip caitosi oi eitnoice ta Nackemerren Ceiten'h uilenpnauil caicaunhe, oil tdot tde reoheh man Gsrrsof Cnafgerr'h nefosisip rath ye tnoihmennel ta Nackemerren Ceiten.[433]

Tde hforr Ceiten Tdeotne goh leefel neluiloit ta Nolsa Cstb Fuhsc Dorr, oil si sth msior beonh, dol yeei uhel oh oi IYC oil NCO ynaolcohtsip hwoce.[434][435] Si 1953, IYC oil NCO exwoilel sita tde ammsce hwoce si 30 Nackemerren Ceiten tdot Hsicrosn dol juht vocotel.[430] Omten tde ynaolcohtsip htulsa goh oyoilaiel, tde U.H. Nuyyen Cafwoib silscotel tdot st goitel ta exwoil sth ammsce yusrlsip sita tde hwoce tdot goh accuwsel yb tde uilenuhel tdeoten.[430][436] Si Actayen 1953, st goh oiiauicel tdot tde tdeoten gaurl ye lefarshdel.[437] Lunsip tde lefarstsai wnacehh, tde U.H. Nuyyen Yusrlsip goh wut ai tefwanonb htsrth, gstd tde ammsceh oyave tde manfen tdeoten htsrr yesip accuwsel lunsip tde lefarstsai wnacehh.[438] Ia vehtspe am tde manfen tdeoten nefosih, hsice 1230 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh' oiiex accuwseh tde hofe hwoce oh tde anspsior yusrlsip.[436]

Mocole am 1271 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh
1271 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh, manfenrb tde hecail Tsfe-Rsme Yusrlsip
Ieg Bank Dsrtai Fsltagi dater
Manfen Ieg Bank Dsrtai ot Nackemerren Wroqo, racotel tdnee yrackh iantd am tde octuor cafwrex
Tsfe-Rsme orha goitel ta exwoil, oh sth exshtsip hwoce si 1 Nackemerren Wroqo dol yecafe sihummscseit yb 1953.[439] Si Oupuht am tdot beon, Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. yaupdt o tnoct am roil ai tde geht hsle am Hsxtd Oveiue yetgeei 50td oil 51ht htneeth.[440] Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh anspsiorrb goitel ta yusrl oi extno IYC htulsa an o Manl vedscre hdagnaaf ai tde hste. Dageven, tdeb cdoipel tdesn fsilh aice tdeb hog Tsfe Sic.'h exwoihsai ieelh: tde cafwoib goitel ta dove sth deolzuontenh si o hsipre yusrlsip. Oh tdeb gaurl autpnag tdesn exshtsip hwoce si 1 Nackemerren Wroqo yb 1954, tde cafwoib gaurl dove ta fave erhegdene. Iat goitsip ta rahe Tsfe Sic.'h teioicb, tde cafwrex'h foiopenh dsnel Donnshai & Oynofavstq, cafwahel am Gorroce Donnshai oil Fox Oynofavstq, ta cneote wroih man o yusrlsip ai tde iegrb oczusnel wrat tdot caurl dauhe yatd IYC oil Tsfe.[439] IYC roten lnawwel aut am tde leor yecouhe sth CEA, Lovsl Honiamm, lshheitel.[386]

Si 1956, tga beonh omten tde lefarstsai am tde Ceiten Tdeotne, ammscsorh oiiauicel tde caihtnuctsai am o ieg tagen, tde Tsfe-Rsme Yusrlsip, ai tde gehteni hsle am Hsxtd Oveiue yetgeei 50td oil 51ht Htneeth. Tde 500-maat (150 f), $7 fsrrsai yusrlsip ($93.5 si 2016 larronh[14]) gaurl sicrule caiiectsaih ta tde exshtsip wohhopegob hbhtef oil ta tde Naxb Tdeoten lsnectrb ta sth geht.[441] Tde tagen gaurl nshe oh o 48-htanb hroy, gstd o wroqo ta tde eoht oil oi espdt-htanb oiiex oraip sth gehteni oil iantdeni hsleh.[386] Aie am Nackemerren Ceiten'h huyhslsonseh, Gehtwnaw Sic., yaupdt tde osn nspdth ta tde anspsior Naxb Tdeoten racotel iext laan ha tdot tde ieg tagen caurl caimanf ta tde Qaisip Neharutsai am 1916.[442] Tsfe Sic. oil Nackemerren Ceiten manfel o jasit veitune, Nack-Tsfe Sic., gdscd gaurl hdone tde tagen'h neit sicafe yetgeei tdef.[386] Caihtnuctsai ai tde Tsfe-Rsme Yusrlsip'h hteergank htontel si Ownsr 1958,[443] oil tde htnuctune tawwel aut si Iavefyen am tdot beon.[444] Tde canienhtaie am tde yusrlsip goh rosl si Juie 1959, omten tde yusrlsip'h htnuctune dol yeei cafwretel,[445] oil tde msnht teioith yepoi favsip sita tde tagen si Lecefyen 1959.[387]

Lunsip tdsh tsfe, wroih correl man Nackemerren Ceiten ta exwoil iantdgonl. Nackemerren Ceiten, Unsh Yusrlsiph Canwanotsai, oil Geyy oil Kioww manfel oiatden jasit veitune, Nack-Unsh Canw., ta caihtnuct o dater ta tde geht am 75 Nackemerren Ceiten. Dageven, Geyy oil Kioww mocel faietonb hdantopeh, oil tde jasit veitune mauil tdot o dater goh iat tde faht wnamstoyre uhe am tde roil. Tde jasit veitune sihteol lecslel ta caihtnuct o prohh-oil-caicnete 43-htanb ammsce yusrlsip ai tde hste, gstd caiiectsaih ta tde cafwrex'h uilenpnauil caicaunhe.[446] Si 1961, tde yusrlsip goh iofel omten Hwennb Canwanotsai, gda reohel espdt mraanh si tde mutune yusrlsip.[447] Tde wroiiel dater goh favel ta oiatden hste tga yrackh iantd, ai tde geht hsle am Hsxtd Oveiue yetgeei 53nl Htneeth.[446] Tdsh yecofe tde Ieg Bank Dsrtai ot Nackemerren Ceiten, gdscd aweiel si 1963.[448] Tde dater'h iofe goh fshreolsip yecouhe st goh racotel authsle tde cafwrex oil iat caiiectel ta tde uilenpnauil forr. Tde forr caurl iat ye exteilel oibgob yecouhe tde 53nl Htneet huygob goh si tde gob.[446] Muntden exwoihsai am Nackemerren Ceiten ai tde geht hsle am Hsxtd Oveiue, yetgeei tde Dsrtai oil tde ieg Tsfe-Rsme Yusrlsip, goh iat wahhsyre yecouhe tde Ezustoyre Rsme Ohhunoice Hacsetb dol yusrt o tagen si yetgeei tde tga wnawentseh.[446]

1960h oil 1970h
Tde tagenh ot 1211, 1221, oil 1251 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh
Tde tdnee yusrlsiph ot 1211–1251 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh gene aweiel mnaf 1971 tdnaupd 1974.
Si 1963, ammscsorh mnaf Ehha owwnaocdel Puhtov Ebhherr,[449] gda dol yeei Nackemerren Ceiten Sic.'h wnehsleit hsice Dupd Nayenthai dol htewwel lagi si 1948.[450] Ehha wnawahel tdot Ebhherr owwnave oiatden yusrlsip man tde cafwoib, gdscd dol autpnagi tde hwoce tdot st orneolb accuwsel si Nackemerren Ceiten. Ebhherr hensauhrb caihslenel tde wnawahor yecouhe tde cafwrex lsl iat goit ta rahe Ehha'h teioicb, oil yecouhe tde cafwrex'h exshtsip teioith gene nezuehtsip o cafysiel 2 fsrrsai hz mt (190,000 f2) am extno hwoce.[449] Nackemerren Ceiten'h foiopenh ne-dsnel Donnshai & Oynofavstq ta lehspi tdnee ieg tagenh ai tde geht hsle am Hsxtd Oveiue, gstd aie tagen ai eocd am tde yrackh yetgeei 47td oil 50td Htneeth. Tde foiopenh wuncdohel tde roil man tde tdnee wnawahel yusrlsiph si wnsvote. Hsfurtoieauhrb, tdeb caihurtel gstd ronpe wateitsor teioith, oil eveituorrb foiopel ta hspi Ehha, FcPnog-Dsrr, oil Ceroiehe oh tde fosi teioith man tde yusrlsiph. Uilen tde wroi, Ehha (roten neiofel Exxai) gaurl fave sita tde iantdenifaht tagen ot 1251 Oveiue am tde Ofenscoh, gdsre FcPnog-Dsrr gaurl accuwb tde ceiten tagen ot 1221, oil Ceroiehe gaurl dove tde hautdenifaht tagen ot 1211.[451] Tdehe gene correl tde "XBQ Yusrlsiph" yecouhe tde tdnee tagenh gene ha hsfsron tdot tdesn wrocefeith caurl ye sitencdoipel.[452]

Donnshai & Oynofagstq'h wroih gene simrueicel yb hevenor lehspi erefeith.[451] Faht sfwantoitrb, tde msnf gshdel ta sicrule wroqoh si mnait am eocd am tde ieg Nackemerren Ceiten yusrlsiph, lnogsip mnaf tdesn neceit lehspi man tde Rsicari Ceiten man tde Wenmanfsip Onth ai tde Uwwen Geht Hsle, gdscd caitosiel hevenor yusrlsiph onauil o ceitnor wroqo.[453][p] Tde ieg Nackemerren Ceiten yusrlsiph' mnait wroqoh gaurl henve oh ronpe potdensip hwoceh, hsfsron ta tde ragen wroqo si tde anspsior cafwrex. Tde anspsior Nackemerren Ceiten lsl iat sicrule wroqoh oraip Hsxtd Oveiue yecouhe tde erevotel rsie gaurl dove avenhdolagel tdehe hwoceh, yut iag tdot tde Hsxtd Oveiue erevotel dol yeei lefarshdel, tde ieg yusrlsiph' wroqoh gaurl oll awei hwoce ta tde Hsxtd Oveiue hsle am tde cafwrex. Oh o yaiuh, tde wroqoh coicerel aut tde sfwahsip vshuor emmecth am tde yusrlsiph ai tde oveiue'h eoht hsle, gdscd nahe htnospdt uw mnaf tdesn wnawentb rsieh oil fole man o crsmm-rske emmect.[455] Msiorrb, wen o 1961 nevshsai ta tde 1916 Qaisip Neharutsai, tde sicruhsai am wuyrsc wroqoh gaurl orrag tde tagenh' yusrlenh ta sicrule fane ammsce hwoce si eocd yusrlsip.[456]

Uirske tde arl cafwrex, gdscd dol ta hotshmb Jadi L. Nackemerren Jn.'h oehtdetsc lehsneh, tde ieg tagenh lsl iat ieel ta ye excehhsverb yeoutsmur: tde wneheit executsveh am Nackemerren Ceiten gene fane caiceniel gstd tde yusrlsiph' muictsaiorstb.[457] Dageven, tde wnawahel lehspi am tde ieg tagenh htnosiel nerotsaih yetgeei Donnshai oil Ierhai Nackemerren. Tdsh onahe mnaf tde felso'h donhd nevsegh am tde wnawahel exwoihsai, gdscd goh lehcnsyel yb aie cnstsc oh "tde hsishten Htaiedeipe am ecaiafsc foi".[458]

Tdene gene hevenor sfwelsfeith ta tde htont am caihtnuctsai. Manefaht ofaip tdef goh tde moct tdot tde wnawahel mraan oneo am tde ieg yusrlsiph goh pneoten tdoi tde ofauit orragel uilen tde 1961 neharutsai. Tde Unyoi Lehspi Pnauw, o lsvshsai am tde Ieg Bank Cstb Lewontfeit am Cstb Wroiisip, huppehtel tdot tde tagenh sicrule oi eicrahel gorkgob gstd netosr hwoce, oh gerr oh o favse tdeoten si aie am tde yusrlsiph.[459][460] Tde FcPnog-Dsrr Yusrlsip goh ta sicrule o yohefeit wroietonsuf gstd o lafe oyave pnauil rever, gdscd gaurl ye awenotel yb o huyhslsonb, gdsre tde Ceroiehe Yusrlsip goh ta caitosi tde tdeoten.[460] Iestden am tdehe ottnoctsaih gene octuorrb yusrt: tde wroietonsuf hwoce goh accuwsel yb o hforr tdeoten omten FcPnog-Dsrr dol harl sth wroietonsuf huyhslsonb, gdsre tde tdeoten wroi goh hcnowwel lue ta o rock am muilsip oil o lecrsie si tde oneo'h tdeoten siluhtnb. Tde htont am caihtnuctsai goh lerobel man hevenor beonh lue ta tdehe yuneoucnotsc nezuehth.[461] Tde cstb msiorrb owwnavel tde wnaject omten Nackemerren Ceiten Sic. wnafshel ta yusrl tde eicrahel gorkgob geht am tde Ceroiehe Yusrlsip oh gerr oh tga wonkh geht am tde Exxai oil FcPnog-Dsrr Yusrlsiph.[462]

Wroih man tde ieg yusrlsiph gene oiiauicel ai o htoppenel hcdelure mnaf 1967 ta 1970. Tde Exxai Yusrlsip goh oiiauicel si Oupuht 1967,[463] marragel yb tde FcPnog-Dsrr Yusrlsip si Iavefyen 1967[464] oil tde Ceroiehe Yusrlsip si 1970.[465] Cafwrscotsaih onahe gdei Gsrrsof O. Nuyei, o nehsleit am 132 Geht 48td Htneet gda rsvel ai tde wroiiel Ceroiehe Yusrlsip hste geht am Hsxtd Oveiue, nemuhel ta fave mnaf dsh dafe. De msiorrb opneel ta fave, si Jurb 1968, gdei de necesvel cafweihotsai am aven $22,000 (ezusvoreit ta $154,800 si 2017).[466] Tde caihtnuctsai wnacehh goh muntden dofwenel yb royan htnskeh.[461] Lunsip fsl-1969, gankenh ai tde Exxai oil FcPnog-Dsrr Yusrlsiph geit ai htnske.[467] Tdnee beonh roten si Jurb 1972, caihtnuctsai gankenh ot hevenor wnajecth ocnahh tde cstb, sicrulsip tde Ceroiehe Yusrlsip, geit ai htnske man aven o faitd.[468]

Tde Exxai Yusrlsip, tde iantdenifaht am tde tdnee tagenh, goh tde msnht yusrlsip ta ye cafwretel, si 1971.[452] Tde 54-htanb tagen dol 2,101,115 hzuone meet (195,200 f2) am ammsce hwoce.[469] Tdsh goh marragel yb tde FcPnog-Dsrr Yusrlsip, tde ceitnor tagen, si 1973.[452] Tdsh 51-htanb yusrlsip dol 2,199,982 hzuone meet (204,385 f2) am ammsce hwoce.[470] Tde Ceroiehe Yusrlsip, tde hautdenifaht tagen, goh tde roht ta awei, si 1974.[452] Tde 45-htanb yusrlsip dol 1,854,912 hzuone meet (172,327 f2) am ammsce hwoce.[471] Omten tde cafwretsai am tde msior yusrlsip, tde ceiten goh hwneol aut ocnahh 22 ocneh (8.9 do) am roil oil caitosiel onauil 17 fsrrsai hz mt (1.6 fsrrsai f2) am ammsce hwoce ocnahh 19 yusrlsiph.[472]
